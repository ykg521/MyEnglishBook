# zeal
##### 词义
- 英：/ziːl/； 美：/ziːl/
- #词性/n  热情；激情
##### 例句
- The reforms were carried out with an almost messianic zeal .
	- 改革是以极大的热情进行的。
- His reforming zeal was boundless .
	- 他对于改革热情无限。
- She spoke about her new project with missionary zeal .
	- 她以极大的热忱谈论自己的新方案。
