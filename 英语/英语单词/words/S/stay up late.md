# stay up late
##### 词义
- 美：/ steɪ ʌp leɪt /；
- #词性/v  熬夜，晚睡
##### 例句
- I used to stay up late with my mom and watch movies.
	- 我过去常和妈妈熬夜看电影。
- It is bad for health to stay up late .
	- 熬夜对健康有害。
- I am not allowed to stay up late by my parents.
	- 我父母不允许我熬夜。
