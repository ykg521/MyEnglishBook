# sporadically
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  零星；偶发地
##### 例句
- Fighting continued sporadically for two months .
	- 战斗断断续续地进行了两个月。
- She attended lectures only sporadically .
	- 她只是偶尔听听课。
- The militants have been sporadically fighting the government for years .
	- 几年来，反叛分子一直对政府实施零星的战斗。
