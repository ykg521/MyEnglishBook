- #词性/adj  肌肉的；肌肉发达的；强壮的

- As a general rule , all muscular effort is enhanced by breathing in as the effort is made

- Become muscular or fleshy .
	- 变成肌肉的或肉质的。
- He is tall , broad and muscular .
