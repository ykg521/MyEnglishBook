# agonize

- #词性/vi  苦苦思索；焦虑不已
##### 例句
- Patrick was known to agonize over questions of policy .
	- 大家都知道帕特里克喜欢苦苦思索政策方面的问题。
- She only made the decision to apply for training after years of agonizing .
