# beep

## N-COUNT; SOUND
（汽车喇叭、电话答录机等发出的）嘟嘟声，哔哔声 A beep is a short, loud sound like that made by a car horn or a telephone answering machine.
## V-ERG
（使）发出嘟嘟声；（使）发出哔哔声 If something such as a horn beeps, or you beep it, it makes a short, harsh sound.
