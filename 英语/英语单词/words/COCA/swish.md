# swish

## V-ERG
(使)刷地(或嗖地、呼地)快速移动 If something swishes or if you swish it, it moves quickly through the air, making a soft sound.
## ADJ-GRADED
好看的;时髦的;高档的 If you describe something as swish, you mean that it is smart and fashionable.
