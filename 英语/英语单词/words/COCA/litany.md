# litany

## N-COUNT
陈词滥调；枯燥冗长的陈述 If you describe what someone says as a litany of things, you mean that you have heard it many times before, and you think it is boring or insincere.
## N-COUNT
连祷；应答祈祷；启应祈祷 A litany is part of a church service in which the priest says a set group of words and the people reply, also using a set group of words.
