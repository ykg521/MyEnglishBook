# sortie

## N-COUNT
短程旅行(尤指去陌生地方) A sortie is a brief trip away from your home base, especially a trip to an unfamiliar place.
## N-COUNT
(军队的)出击，袭击 If a military force makes a sortie, it leaves its own position and goes briefly into enemy territory to make an attack.
