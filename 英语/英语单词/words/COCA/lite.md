# lite

## ADJ
（食物或饮料）低热量的，低糖的，低脂的；低酒精的 Lite is used to describe foods or drinks that contain few calories or low amounts of sugar, fat, or alcohol.
## ADJ
弱化的；简化的；缩水的 If you describe something as lite, you dislike it because it is a weaker and less demanding version of something else.
