# screech

## VERB
(车辆、汽车轮胎)发出刺耳声 If a vehicle screeches somewhere or if its tyres screech, its tyres make an unpleasant high-pitched noise on the road.
## VERB
尖声喊出 When you screech something, you shout it in a loud, unpleasant, high-pitched voice.
## VERB
(鸟、动物等)尖叫，尖鸣，发出刺耳声 When a bird, animal, or thing screeches, it makes a loud, unpleasant, high-pitched noise.
