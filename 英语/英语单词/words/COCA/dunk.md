# dunk

## VERB
(将饼干、面包等)浸一浸,泡一泡 If you dunk something such as a biscuit or a piece of bread in a drink or in soup, you dip it into the drink or soup before eating it.
## VERB
浸;泡 If you dunk something in a liquid, you put it in the liquid, especially for a particular purpose and for a short time.
