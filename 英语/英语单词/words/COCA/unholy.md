# unholy

## ADJ
极不合理的；极令人厌恶的 You use unholy to emphasize how unreasonable or unpleasant you think something is.
## ADJ
（由通常敌对的两方或多方组成的联盟）不同寻常的，诡异的 If you refer to two or more people or groups working together as an unholy alliance, you mean that this arrangement is unusual because the people usually oppose each other.
## ADJ-GRADED
邪恶的；罪恶的；亵渎的 If you describe something as unholy, you mean that it is wicked or bad.
