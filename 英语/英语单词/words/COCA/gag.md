# gag

## N-COUNT
（使人不能说话的）封口布，塞口物 A gag is something such as a piece of cloth that is tied around or put inside someone's mouth in order to stop them from speaking.
## VERB
（用布）封住（或堵住）…的嘴 If someone gags you, they tie a piece of cloth around your mouth in order to stop you from speaking or shouting.
## VERB
压制…的言论自由；迫使…缄默 If a person is gagged by someone in authority, they are prevented from expressing their opinion or from publishing certain information.
## VERB
噎住；噎得作呕 If you gag, you cannot swallow and nearly vomit.
## N-COUNT
笑话；玩笑；插科打诨 A gag is a joke.
## VERB
渴望拥有；期盼做 If you say that someone is gagging for something or is gagging to do something, you are emphasizing that they want to have it or do it very much.
