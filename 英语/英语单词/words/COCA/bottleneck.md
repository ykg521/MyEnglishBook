# bottleneck

## N-COUNT
瓶颈路段；狭窄路段；交通拥堵地段 A bottleneck is a place where a road becomes narrow or where it meets another road so that the traffic slows down or stops, often causing traffic jams.
## N-COUNT
（阻碍进展的）瓶颈，障碍 A bottleneck is a situation that stops a process or activity from progressing.
