# unease

## N-UNCOUNT
焦虑；不安 If you have a feeling of unease, you feel rather anxious or afraid, because you think that something is wrong.
## N-UNCOUNT
不满；愤怒；紧张 If you say that there is unease in a situation, you mean that people are dissatisfied or angry, but have not yet started to take any action.
