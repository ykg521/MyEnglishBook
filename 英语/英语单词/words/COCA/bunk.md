# bunk

## N-COUNT
（尤指船上或车上的）铺位，卧铺 A bunk is a bed that is fixed to a wall, especially in a ship or caravan.
## N-UNCOUNT
胡说；谎言 If you describe something as bunk, you think that it is foolish or untrue.
## PHRASE
不辞而别；溜走 If you do a bunk, you suddenly leave a place without telling anyone.
