# swab

## N-COUNT
(医用)拭子，棉签，药签 A swab is a small piece of cotton wool used by a doctor or nurse for cleaning a wound or putting a substance on it.
## VERB
(用拖把、抹布等)擦洗，擦拭 If you swab something, you clean it using a wet cloth or a tool called a mop.
