# unforgiving

## ADJ
不宽容的；不原谅人的 If you describe someone as unforgiving, you mean that they are unwilling to forgive other people.
## ADJ-GRADED
（形势或活动）无情的，难以应付的，不利的 If you describe a situation or activity as unforgiving, you mean that it causes a lot of people to experience great difficulty or failure, even people who deserve to succeed.
