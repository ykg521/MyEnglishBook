# anoint

## VERB
（常指在宗教仪式中）涂油（或水）于（人体） To anoint someone means to put oil or water on a part of their body, usually for religious reasons.
## VERB
选定;指定 If a person in a position of authority anoints someone, they choose them to do a particular important job.
