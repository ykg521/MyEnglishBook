# tarmac

## N-UNCOUNT
(铺路面用的)柏油碎石 Tarmac is a material used for making road surfaces, consisting of crushed stones mixed with tar.
## N-SING
柏油碎石地面;(尤指)停机坪 The tarmac is an area with a surface made of tarmac, especially the area from which planes take off at an airport.
