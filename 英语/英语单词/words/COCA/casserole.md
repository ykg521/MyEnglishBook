# casserole

## N-COUNT
(以肉、蔬菜为原料做成的)炖菜，砂锅菜 A casserole is a dish made of meat and vegetables that have been cooked slowly in a liquid.
## N-COUNT
砂锅;炖锅 A casserole or a casserole dish is a large heavy container with a lid. You cook casseroles and other dishes in it.
## VERB
用文火烹煮;炖 If you casserole meat and vegetables, you cook them slowly in a liquid.
