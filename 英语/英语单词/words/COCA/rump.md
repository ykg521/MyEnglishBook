# rump

## N-SING
残余分子;残存力量 The rump of a group, organization, or country consists of the members who remain in it after the rest have left.
## N-COUNT
（动物的）尾部，臀部 An animal's rump is its rear end.
## N-UNCOUNT
臀肉牛排 Rump or rump steak is meat cut from the rear end of a cow.
## N-COUNT
（人的）屁股，臀部 A person's rump is his or her bottom.
