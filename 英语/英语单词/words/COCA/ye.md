# ye

## PRON-PLURAL
你们，汝等，尔辈（you的过时用法，诗歌或宗教用语） Ye is an old-fashioned, poetic, or religious word for you when you are talking to more than one person.
## DET
（模仿the的旧时书面形式） Ye is sometimes used in imitation of an old written form of the word 'the'.
