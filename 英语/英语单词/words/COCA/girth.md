# girth

## N-VAR
（人、动物等的）围长、腰围 The girth of an object, for example a person's or an animal's body, is its width or thickness, considered as the measurement around its circumference.
## N-COUNT
（马的）肚带，鞍带 A girth is a leather strap which is fastened firmly around the middle of a horse to keep the saddle or load in the right place.
