# gondola

## N-COUNT
（尤指威尼斯的）凤尾船，贡多拉 A gondola is a long narrow boat that is used especially in Venice. It has a flat bottom and curves upwards at both ends. A person stands at one end of the boat and uses a long pole to move and steer it.
