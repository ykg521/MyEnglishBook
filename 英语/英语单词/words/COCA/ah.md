# ah

## EXCLAM
（书面语中用于表示在交谈时作出回应或引起他人注意或表示惊讶、失望）啊 Ah is used in writing to represent a noise that people make in conversation, for example to acknowledge or draw attention to something, or to express surprise or disappointment.
