# glint

## VERB
闪闪发光；闪亮 If something glints, it produces or reflects a quick flash of light.
## N-COUNT
闪光；闪烁 A glint is a quick flash of light.
## VERB
（眼睛）闪光，闪动，闪亮 If someone's eyes glint, they shine and express a particular emotion.
## N-SING
（眼神的）闪光，闪动，闪亮 A glint in someone's eyes is a brightness that expresses a particular emotion.
