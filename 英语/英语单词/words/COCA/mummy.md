# mummy

## N-FAMILY
(尤指儿语)妈咪，妈妈 Some people, especially young children, call their mother mummy .
## N-COUNT
木乃伊 A mummy is a dead body which was preserved long ago by being rubbed with special oils and wrapped in cloth.
