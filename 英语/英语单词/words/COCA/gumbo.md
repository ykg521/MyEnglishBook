# gumbo

## N-VAR
秋葵汤（美国南部用肉或鱼以及秋葵荚做的汤或炖菜） Gumbo is a type of soup or stew from the southern United States. It can be made with meat or fish, and usually contains okra.
## N-UNCOUNT
（美国某些地方）同okra In parts of the United States, gumbo is another name for okra.
