# lurid

## ADJ-GRADED
（暴力、性爱等）耸人听闻的，触目惊心的，骇人的 If you say that something is lurid, you are critical of it because it involves a lot of violence, sex, or shocking detail.
## ADJ-GRADED
妖冶的；俗丽的；浓艳的 If you describe something as lurid, you do not like it because it is very brightly coloured.
