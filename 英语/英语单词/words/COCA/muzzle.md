# muzzle

## N-COUNT
(动物的)口鼻部 The muzzle of an animal such as a dog is its nose and mouth.
## N-COUNT
(罩住狗的口鼻的)嘴套，口套 A muzzle is an object that is put over a dog's nose and mouth so that it cannot bite people or make a noise.
## VERB
给(狗等)戴上嘴套 If you muzzle a dog or other animal, you put a muzzle over its nose and mouth.
## VERB
使缄默;迫使…保持沉默 If you say that someone is muzzled, you are complaining that they are prevented from expressing their views freely.
## N-COUNT
枪口;炮口 The muzzle of a gun is the end where the bullets come out when it is fired.
