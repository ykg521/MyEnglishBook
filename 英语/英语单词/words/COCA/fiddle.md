# fiddle

## VERB
摆弄;拨弄 If you fiddle with an object, you keep moving it or touching it with your fingers.
## VERB
作小改动;略微变动;稍稍修改 If you fiddle with something, you change it in minor ways.
## VERB
调校;调整 If you fiddle with a machine, you adjust it.
## VERB
篡改，伪造(财务记录) If someone fiddles financial documents, they alter them dishonestly so that they get money for themselves.
## N-COUNT
欺诈;诈骗 A fiddle is a dishonest action or scheme in which someone gets money for themselves.
## N-SING
麻烦之事;繁琐之事 If something is a fiddle, it is quite difficult to do because it involves small or complicated objects.
## N-VAR
(尤指用于演奏民间音乐的)小提琴 Some people call violins fiddles, especially when they are used to play folk music.
## PHRASE
非常健康;充满活力 Someone who is as fit as a fiddle is very healthy and full of energy.
## PHRASE
干骗钱的勾当;诈骗 If someone is on the fiddle, they get money by doing illegal or dishonest things.
## PHRASE
大难临头仍歌舞升平;见危难而若无其事 If you say that someone is fiddling while Rome burns, you mean that they are not dealing with a difficult or dangerous situation but instead are doing useless things or pretending that nothing is wrong.
## PHRASE
处于次要地位;当副手;唱配角 If you play second fiddle to someone, your position is less important than theirs in something that you are doing together.
