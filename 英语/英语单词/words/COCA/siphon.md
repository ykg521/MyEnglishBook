# siphon

## VERB
通过虹吸管吸(或输送、抽干) If you siphon liquid from a container, you make it come out through a tube and down into a lower container by enabling the pressure of the air on it to push it out.
## N-COUNT
虹吸管 A siphon is a tube that you use for siphoning liquid.
## VERB
抽取，抽出，挪用(金钱或资源) If you siphon money or resources from something, you cause them to be used for a purpose for which they were not intended.
