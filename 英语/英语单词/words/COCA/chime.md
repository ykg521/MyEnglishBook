# chime

## VERB
(铃)响;(钟)报时，敲响 When a bell or a clock chimes, it makes ringing sounds.
## N-COUNT
铃声;(尤指)钟声 A chime is a ringing sound made by a bell, especially when it is part of a clock.
## N-PLURAL
风铃 Chimes are a set of small objects which make a ringing sound when they are blown by the wind.
