# lacy

## ADJ-GRADED
网眼织物的；蕾丝做的；带蕾丝的 Lacy things are made from lace or have pieces of lace attached to them.
## ADJ-GRADED
（尤指因娇柔）呈蕾丝状的，似蕾丝的 Lacy is used to describe something that looks like lace, especially because it is very delicate.
