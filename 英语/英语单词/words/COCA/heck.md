# heck

## EXCLAM
（表示稍感恼怒、吃惊等）见鬼，糟糕 People sometimes say 'heck!' when they are slightly irritated or surprised.
## PHRASE
极大;极多 People use a heck of to emphasize how big something is or how much of it there is.
## PHRASE
（尤在迷惑或恼怒时用于疑问句中表示强调）到底，究竟 You use the heck in expressions such as 'what the heck' and 'how the heck' in order to emphasize a question, especially when you are puzzled or annoyed.
## PHRASE
管它呢;不管它 You say 'what the heck' to indicate that you do not care about a bad aspect of an action or situation.
