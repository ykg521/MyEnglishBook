# patter

## VERB
嗒嗒作响；嗒嗒地拍 If something patters on a surface, it hits it quickly several times, making quiet, tapping sounds.
## N-SING
急促的轻拍声；嗒嗒声 A patter is a series of quick, quiet, tapping sounds.
## N-SING
（通常以娱乐、推销或劝说为目的的）顺口溜，伶俐话，连珠炮似的叫卖语 Someone's patter is a series of things that they say quickly and easily, usually in order to entertain people or to persuade them to buy or do something.
