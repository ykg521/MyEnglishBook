# unfathomable

## ADJ-GRADED
（事物）高深莫测的，深奥的 If you describe something as unfathomable, you mean that it cannot be understood or explained, usually because it is very strange or complicated.
## ADJ
（人或表情）难以捉摸的 If you use unfathomable to describe a person or the expression on their face, you mean that you cannot tell what they are thinking or what they intend to do.
