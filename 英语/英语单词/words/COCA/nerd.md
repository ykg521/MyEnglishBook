# nerd

## N-COUNT
(尤指穿着落伍或过于迷恋计算机或科学的)蠢人，(书)呆子 If you say that someone is a nerd, you mean that they are stupid or ridiculous, especially because they wear unfashionable clothes or show too much interest in computers or science.
