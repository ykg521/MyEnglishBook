# valet

## N-COUNT
（男子的）贴身男仆,男仆从 A valet is a male servant who looks after his employer by doing things such as caring for his clothes and cooking for him.
## VERB
(受雇）洗（车） If someone valets a vehicle, they are paid to clean it thoroughly inside and out.
