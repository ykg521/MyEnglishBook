# pharmacist

## N-COUNT
药剂师；药商 A pharmacist is a person who is qualified to prepare and sell medicines.
## N-COUNT
药店；药房 A pharmacist or a pharmacist's is a shop in which drugs and medicines are sold by a pharmacist.
