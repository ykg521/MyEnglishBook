# moot

## VERB
提出(计划、主意或主题)供讨论 If a plan, idea, or subject is mooted, it is suggested or introduced for discussion.
## ADJ-GRADED
有争议的;尚未达成一致的;未决的 If something is a moot point or question, people cannot agree about it.
