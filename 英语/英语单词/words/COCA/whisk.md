# whisk

## VERB
迅速送走;匆匆带走 If you whisk someone or something somewhere, you take them or move them there quickly.
## VERB
搅拌，搅打(鸡蛋、奶油等) If you whisk something such as eggs or cream, you stir it very fast, often with an electric device, so that it becomes full of small bubbles.
## N-COUNT
搅拌器;打蛋器 A whisk is a kitchen tool used for whisking eggs or cream.
