# bastard

## N-COUNT
杂种；坏蛋；混蛋 Bastard is an insulting word which some people use about a person, especially a man, who has behaved very badly.
## N-COUNT
（走运或倒霉的）家伙 Some people say things such as lucky bastard or poor bastard to refer to a person, usually a man, who they think is very lucky, or very unlucky.
## N-COUNT
私生子（可能具冒犯意味） A bastard is a person whose parents were not married to each other at the time that he or she was born. This use could cause offence.
## N-SING
极讨厌的事；极麻烦的事 If someone describes a problem or a situation as a bastard, they mean that it is extremely annoying or difficult to deal with.
