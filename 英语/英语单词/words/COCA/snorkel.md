# snorkel

## N-COUNT
(潜水者使用的)通气管，水下呼吸管 A snorkel is a tube through which a person swimming just under the surface of the sea can breathe.
## VERB
用通气管潜泳 When someone snorkels, they swim under water using a snorkel.
