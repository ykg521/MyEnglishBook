# ladle

## N-COUNT
长柄勺 A ladle is a large, round, deep spoon with a long handle, used for serving soup, stew, or sauce.
## VERB
（尤指用长柄勺）舀，盛 If you ladle food such as soup or stew, you serve it, especially with a ladle.
