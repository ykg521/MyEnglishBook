# presbyterian

## ADJ
长老制的，长老会的（尤指苏格兰和美国的新教教会） Presbyterian means belonging or relating to a Protestant church, found especially in Scotland or the United States, which is governed by a body of official people all of equal rank.
## N-COUNT
长老会教徒 A Presbyterian is a member of the Presbyterian church.
