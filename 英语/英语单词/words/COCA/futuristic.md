# futuristic

## ADJ-GRADED
未来派的;极其前卫现代的 Something that is futuristic looks or seems very modern and unusual, like something from the future.
## ADJ
(电影或小说)幻想未来的 A futuristic film or book tells a story that is set in the future, when things are different.
