# cluck

## VERB
(母鸡)咯咯叫 When a hen clucks, it makes short, low noises.
## VERB
（对…）大惊小怪，过分关心 To cluck over someone or something means to behave in a fussy or protective way.
## VERB
发咂咂声(以示不满) To cluck at someone or something means to make disapproving noises or say things in a disapproving way.
