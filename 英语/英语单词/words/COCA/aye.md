# aye

## CONVENTION
是，对，同意（用于英国英语方言中） Aye means yes; used in some dialects of British English.
## ADV
赞成票 If you vote aye, you vote in favour of something.
## N-PLURAL
投赞成票的人 The ayes are the people who vote in favour of something.
