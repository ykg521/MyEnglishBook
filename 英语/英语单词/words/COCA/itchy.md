# itchy

## ADJ-GRADED
发痒的;令人感到瘙痒的 If a part of your body or something you are wearing is itchy, you have an unpleasant feeling on your skin that makes you want to scratch.
## PHRASE
漫游的渴望;旅行的渴望 If you have itchy feet, you have a strong desire to leave a place and to travel.
