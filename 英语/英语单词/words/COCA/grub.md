# grub

## N-COUNT
（昆虫的）幼虫，蛆 A grub is a young insect which has just come out of an egg and looks like a short fat worm.
## N-UNCOUNT
食物；吃的 Grub is food.
## VERB
翻找；搜寻 If you grub around, you search for something.
