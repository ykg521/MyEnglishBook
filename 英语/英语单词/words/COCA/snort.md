# snort

## VERB
(人或牲畜)喷鼻息;(人表示不赞成、可笑等)哼鼻子，嗤之以鼻 When people or animals snort, they breathe air noisily out through their noses. People sometimes snort in order to express disapproval or amusement.
## VERB
(表示轻蔑)哼着鼻子说 If someone snorts something, they say it in a way that shows contempt.
## VERB
吸食(毒品) To snort a drug such as cocaine means to breathe it in quickly through your nose.
