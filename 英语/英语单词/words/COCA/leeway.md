# leeway

## N-UNCOUNT
自由；自由空间；回旋余地 Leeway is the freedom that someone has to take the action they want to or to change their plans.
## N-UNCOUNT
（需要弥补的）失去时间，落后差距 If you have leeway to make up, you have to work hard because you do not have much time to reach a particular goal.
