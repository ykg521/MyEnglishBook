# laminated

## ADJ
由薄片叠成的；层压的；夹层的 Material such as wood or plastic that is laminated consists of several thin sheets or layers that are stuck together.
## ADJ
覆有薄片护层的；（尤指）覆有透明（或有色）塑料薄膜的 A product that is laminated is covered with a thin sheet of something, especially clear or coloured plastic, in order to protect it.
