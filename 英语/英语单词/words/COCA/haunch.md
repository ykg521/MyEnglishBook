# haunch

## PHRASE
以蹲姿 If you get down on your haunches, you lower yourself towards the ground so that your legs are bent under you and you are balancing on your feet.
## N-COUNT
（动物的）腰腿;（人的）臀胯部 The haunches of an animal or person are the area of the body which includes the bottom, the hips, and the tops of the legs.
