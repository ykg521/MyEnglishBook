# entirely

## ADV
完全地;完整地;全部地 Entirely means completely and not just partly.
## ADV
非常;很;完全 Entirely is also used to emphasize what you are saying.
## ADV
（尤用于表示批评时）不是特别，并非完全 People sometimes use the expression not entirely to reduce the force of a strong statement, especially a critical one.
