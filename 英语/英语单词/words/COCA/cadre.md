# cadre

## N-COUNT
骨干队伍；基干人员 A cadre is a small group of people who have been specially chosen, trained, and organized for a particular purpose.

(尤指共产党的)党务工作者，干部 In some political parties, especially the Communist Party, a cadre is a party worker or official.
