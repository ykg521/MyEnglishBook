# encore

## N-COUNT
（应观众要求的）加演节目 An encore is a short extra performance at the end of a longer one, which an entertainer gives because the audience asks for it.
## VERB
加演节目；返场 If an entertainer encores, they perform an encore.
## CONVENTION
再来一个,再奏一曲（古典音乐会观众要求加演节目时用语） 'Encore' is the word shouted by classical concert audiences when they want a performer to perform an encore.
