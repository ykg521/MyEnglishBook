# leech

## N-COUNT
水蛭；蚂蟥 A leech is a small animal which looks like a worm and lives in water. Leeches feed by attaching themselves to other animals and sucking their blood.
## N-COUNT
吸血鬼；寄生虫 If you describe someone as a leech, you disapprove of them because they deliberately depend on other people, often making money out of them.
