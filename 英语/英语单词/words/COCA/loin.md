# loin

## N-PLURAL
耻骨区；私处；（尤指）性器官 Someone's loins are the front part of their body between their waist and legs, especially their sexual parts.
## N-VAR
（动物的）腰肉，脊背肉 Loin or a loin is a piece of meat which comes from the back or sides of an animal, quite near the tail end.
## PHRASE
准备采取（困难或危险的）行动 If you gird your loins, you prepare to do something difficult or dangerous.
