# 二. 理解了简单句，你就理解了一切
## 2.1 主谓宾定状补
#### 句子成分:主语(Subject)
主语(Subject):句子的主体话题 or 动作的发出者——名词属性

Boys play football. 

Running every morning helps me stay fit.

#### 句子成分:宾语(Object)
宾语(0bject):谓语动词的执行对象——名词属性

She ate sushi.

He gave his sister a birthday gift,

I enjoy reading books.

#### 句子成分:定语(Attributive)
定语(Attributive)：修饰名词性成分的成分——形容词属性

The red car is mine.

The book that iborrowed from the library was really interesting

The man with the hat is my uncle.

#### 句子成分:状语(Adverbial)
状语(Adverbial):修饰动词、形容词、其他副词的成分——副词属性

I will meet you tomorrow.

will meet you at the park.

#### 句子成分:补语(Complement)
补语(Complement):补充说明的额外成分——名词或形容词属性

补语 =宾语补语 or 主语补语（A is B)

She painted the room <font style="background-color:#FBDE28;">blue</font>. 【room was blue】

We consider him <font style="background-color:#DF2A3F;">a fool</font>. 【he is a fool】

I found the movie to <font style="background-color:#FBDE28;">be very interesting</font>. 【movie was interesting】

You should leave the door <font style="background-color:#FBDE28;">open</font>. 【door is open】

He is walking around <font style="background-color:#FBDE28;">naked</font>. 【he is naked】

I asked him <font style="background-color:#DF2A3F;">to buy something for me</font>.【he was to buy something for me】
