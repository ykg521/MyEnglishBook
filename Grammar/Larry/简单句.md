## 2.2 简单句的构成 + 2.3 语法分析实践（第一弹）
简单句的构成
1、SV（主谓）：主语+谓语动词
It happens.
He <mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-yellow" data-id="c867374d-5709-4e01-89db-0e4c2501d781">came</mark>.
The girl with a black bag on her back <mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-yellow" data-id="3929e761-e578-4c23-852b-8607203766f0">smiled</mark>.
The airplane that starts from Hongkong at noon <mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-yellow" data-id="6f1efbca-02fe-41af-91b1-baaf47960edd">is arriving</mark> here at 4 p.m.

2、SVP（主系表）：主语+系动词+表语
Sally is beautiful.
She is <mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-green" data-id="f79b7112-b8cf-4b0b-9b83-4ce06603aa7c">happy</mark>.
He became a teacher when he was 21 years old.
Jimmy's parents <mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-pink" data-id="b9d3b4b5-ef37-4b9c-847e-d2d5ea14996c">felt</mark> <mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-green" data-id="70cf3573-ace8-4381-a867-bb36c2dfff92">really angry</mark> when knowing that he had failed his test
3、SVO (主谓宾）：主语+谓语动词+宾语　
Cats love fish.
4、SVOO（主谓双宾）：主语+谓语动词+间接宾语+直接宾语
He gave me a gift.
5、SVOC（主谓宾补）：主语+谓语动词+宾语+宾语补语
I find the movie interesting
