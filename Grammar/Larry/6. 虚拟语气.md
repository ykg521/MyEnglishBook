## 名词性从包中的虚拟语气【表示意愿】
如果名词性从句中含有特定的、表示强烈主观意志的标志性单词，则从句的谓语动词必须使用原形形式（英式英语使用should+动比类表示强烈主观意志的标志性单词大多表示愿望、建议、命令、请求等，这类标志词包括但不限于：ask，advise，com beg,request,suggest,demand,decide,deserve,desire,insist,order,prefer,propose,urge.

Michael insisted <font style="color:#ea8033;">that this marriage be ended as soon as possible</font>.
Jimmy demanded <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Sally not beallowed to takeany property</font> because she was at fault forgetting pregnant with Michael's child.
Sally suggested <font style="color:#ea8033;">that they start a new life in the slums</font> given their dire financial situation.
Max asked <font style="color:#ea8033;">that his father divorce Sally to remarry Susan</font> because he was fed up withdeliverywork. 
<mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-blue" data-id="2bf6be42-c0b4-4ba9-8a48-cd6f206ebb5b">一定要区别相关词汇是否表达了真实的、强烈的主观意志</mark>
Sally suggested <font style="color:#ea8033;">that they start a new life in the slums</font> given their dire financial situation.
Sally's unusual behavior suggested that she had been pregnant with Michael's child for quite some time.
Michael insisted <font style="color:#ea8033;">that this marriage be ended as soon as possible</font>.
Susan insisted that it was Michael who was at fault.

标志词: ask, advise, command, beg, reguest, suggest, demand, decide, deserve, desire, insist, order, prefer, propose, urge..
要点：①熟悉上述标志词：②判断上述标志词是否具备虚拟意义；③标志词的各种变体，要具备分辨能力。
The attorney advised <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Sally take no property</font> because she was at fault for getting pregnant with Michael's child.
It is advisable <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Sally take no property</font> because she was at fault for getting pregnant with Michael'schild.
The attorney thought it advisable <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Sally take no property</font> because she was at fault for getting pregnant with Michael'schild.
What is advisable is <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Sally take no property</font> because she was at fault for getting pregnant with Michael's child.
Sally was advised <font style="color:#ea8033;">that she take no property</font> because she was at fault for getting pregnant with Michael'schild
The advice <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Sally take no property</font> because she was at fault for getting pregnant with his child makes a lot of sense.

It is + adj + that 结构的虚拟语气. 标志词：important, necessary, advisable, appropriate, desirable, essential, imperative, normal, surprising, strange, vital等。
It is important <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Jimmy make sure Sally doesn't take away any property</font>.
It is strange <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Michael should divorce Susan, the boss of Apple</font>. [表示“竞然”的should需要保留]
It is ... <font style="color:#ea8033;">that ...</font>
It is natural <font style="color:#ea8033;">that people divorce someone they don't love</font>.
It is natural that Michael has divorced Susan.
It is surprising <font style="color:#ea8033;">that Jimmy should marry Susan</font>. [表示“竞然”的should需要保留]
It is surprising that Jimmy has married Susan.

### I wish + 宾语从句：
针对过去的虚拟

I wish <font style="color:#ea8033;">(that) my mom had never broken up with Tancent's boss</font>.
针对现在的虚拟

### wish 和 hope 用法的不同:
<mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-blue" data-id="93ab0be5-7374-454c-9a3c-465f6fcae4dc">相同点①：都可以接不定式</mark>
I wish/hope to marry you soon.
<mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-blue" data-id="95b263bd-e0ec-4b6f-847c-30fb062cd66d">相同点②：都可以for sth</mark>
I wish/hope for the best.

<mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-blue" data-id="3341f0d1-5400-44c2-a01d-73e1f2efaf6f">不同点①：wish sb to do sth成立，但hope sb to do sth不成立</mark>
I wish my dad to get back together with Apple's boss.
<mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-blue" data-id="4f71b0f1-7440-4588-8bfe-e88773b455f6">不同点②：wish可以接双宾语，但hope不可以</mark>
I wish you a better life.
<mark class="eme-highlight eme-h-blue" data-id="63e7dbc8-e582-42ee-a5c3-9029b44490e1">不同点②：wish后接that从句使用虚拟语气，而hope后接that从句使用陈述语气</mark>
I wish <font style="color:#ea8033;">(that) I could marry you</font>.
I hope (that) I can marry you.
