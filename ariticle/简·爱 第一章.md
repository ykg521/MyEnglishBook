We had been wandering, indeed, in the leafless shrubbery an hour in the morning; but since dinner (Mrs. Reed, when there was no company, dined early) the cold winter wind had brought with it clouds so [[sombre]], and a rain so penetrating, that further outdoor exercise was now out of the question.
## 第一句

> There was no possibility of taking a walk that day.
### 结构

- **There be 句型**：There was + 名词
    
    - There was no possibility …… **不存在…… 的可能性**
    
- **of taking a walk**：介词短语作后置定语，修饰 possibility
    
    - of + 动名词（doing）：…… 的可能性
    
- **that day**：时间状语，那天
### 翻译

那天根本不可能出去散步。

---

## 第二句（长句，拆成两部分）

> We had been wandering, indeed, in the leafless shrubbery an hour in the morning;

### 语法

- **had been wandering**：**过去完成进行时**
    
    - had been + doing
    - 表示：**过去某时之前一直在做某事**
    - 这里：午饭前，我们已经在灌木丛里逛了一小时了。
    
- **indeed**：插入语，的确
- **in the leafless shrubbery**：地点状语
    
    - leafless：无叶的（形容词作前置定语）
    
- **an hour / in the morning**：时间状语

### 翻译

的确，早上我们还在光秃秃的灌木丛里逛了一个小时。

---

## 第三句（最复杂）

> but since dinner (Mrs. Reed, when there was no company, dined early)
> 
> the cold winter wind had brought with it clouds so sombre, and a rain so penetrating,
> 
> that further outdoor exercise was now out of the question.

### 1. 从句与插入语

- **since dinner**：时间状语，**自从午饭后**
- **(Mrs. Reed, when there was no company, dined early)**
    
    - 括号里是**插入语**，补充说明
    - when there was no company：时间状语从句
        
        - 没人做客时
        
    - dined early：谓语，**吃得早**
    

### 2. 主句主干

> the cold winter wind had brought with it clouds … and a rain …

- **had brought**：**过去完成时**
    
    - 表示：在 “没法出门” 之前，风就已经带来了……
    
- **with it**：伴随状语，**随之**
- **clouds so sombre, and a rain so penetrating**
    
    - 结构：**名词 + 形容词后置**
        
        - clouds so sombre = sombre clouds 阴沉的云
        - a rain so penetrating = penetrating rain 刺骨的雨
        
    - so 在这里加强形容词，**如此……**
    

### 3. 结果状语从句

> … that further outdoor exercise was now out of the question.

- **so … that …** 句型：**如此…… 以至于……**
    
    - 云如此阴沉，雨如此刺骨 → 以至于不能再出门
    
- **further outdoor exercise**：主语，进一步的户外活动
- **was out of the question**：固定短语
    
    - **不可能，谈不上**
    

---

## 整段语法总结

1. **There be** 表存在
2. **过去完成进行时 had been doing**：过去一直在做
3. **过去完成时 had done**：过去某动作之前已完成
4. **插入语**：补充信息，不影响主句结构
5. **so + 形容词 + 名词 /so … that …**：如此…… 以至于……
6. **of + doing** 作定语
7. **out of the question**：固定搭配，意为 “不可能”

**I was glad of it**

- I：主语（S）
- was：系动词（V）
- glad：表语（Pred）
- of it：介词短语作原因状语

## 2. 第二分句

**I never liked long walks, especially on chilly afternoons**

- I：主语
- never：副词作状语（否定频度）
- liked：谓语动词
- long walks：宾语
- especially on chilly afternoons：介词短语作**时间状语**

## 3. 完全倒装句（重点）

**dreadful to me was the coming home in the raw twilight**

正常语序：

**The coming home in the raw twilight was dreadful to me**

- dreadful：表语（提前倒装）
- to me：介词短语，状语
- was：系动词
- the coming home：主语（动名词短语作主语）
- in the raw twilight：介词短语作**后置定语**，修饰 the coming home

**with nipped fingers and toes**

- with：介词
- nipped fingers and toes：宾语
- nipped：过去分词作前置定语

## 5. 并列过去分词作定语

**and a heart saddened by… and humbled by…**

- saddened by the chidings of Bessie：过去分词短语作**后置定语**
- the nurse：同位语，解释 Bessie
- and humbled by the consciousness…：并列过去分词短语，同样作定语
- of my physical inferiority：介词短语作后置定语
- to Eliza, John, and Georgiana Reed：介词短语作状语

### 1. The said Eliza, John, and Georgiana were now clustered round their mama in the drawing-room.

- **The said + 人名**

- **lay reclined**
    
    lay（lie 的过去式）+ 过去分词 reclined
    
    意为：**躺着，斜靠着**
- **on a sofa**：地点状语
- **by the fireside**：壁炉边（后置定语）

- **主干：she … looked perfectly happy**
- **with her darlings about her**

## 第二段（超级长难句）

### 1. Me, she had dispensed from joining the group.

- **倒装强调**
    
    正常：She had dispensed **me** from joining the group.
- **dispense sb from doing sth**
    
    固定搭配：**免除某人做某事；不让某人做某事**

### 2. saying, “She regretted to be under the necessity of keeping me at a distance…”

- **saying**：现在分词作**伴随状语**
- **under the necessity of doing**
    
    固定短语：**不得不做，必须做**
- **keep me at a distance**：**疏远我，对我冷淡**

### 3. but that until she heard from Bessie, and could discover by her own observation, that …

- **until 引导时间状语从句**
    
    直到…… 之前
- **hear from Bessie**：从贝茜那里得知
- **by her own observation**：通过她自己的观察

### 4. that I was endeavouring in good earnest to acquire …

- **that 引导宾语从句**（作 discover 的宾语）
- **endeavour to do**：努力做……
- **in good earnest**：认真地，真诚地（状语）
- **acquire a … disposition**：养成…… 性情

### 5. a more sociable and childlike disposition, a more attractive and sprightly manner

- 两个并列名词短语，作 acquire 的**并列宾语**

### 6. — something lighter, franker, more natural, as it were —

- **something** 是前面 disposition/manner 的**同位语**
- **as it were**：插入语，**可以说，仿佛**

### 7. she really must exclude me from privileges …

- **exclude sb from sth**：把某人排除在…… 之外
- **privileges intended only for …**
    
    过去分词短语作**后置定语**
    
    = privileges which are intended only for…

## 1. What does Bessie say I have done? I asked.

- **What does Bessie say**：主句，一般现在时疑问结构
    
    - does：助动词
    - say：谓语动词
    
- **I have done**：**宾语从句**，作 say 的宾语
    
    - 原语序：Bessie says (that) I have done what.
    
- **I asked**：主句，叙述句，一般过去时

**结构：**

特殊疑问词 + 助动词 + 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语从句

## 2. I don’t like cavillers or questioners

- I：主语
- don’t like：谓语（一般现在时否定）
- cavillers or questioners：宾语
    
    - cavillers：爱挑剔的人
    - questioners：爱追问的人

## 3. besides, there is something truly forbidding in a child taking up her elders in that manner

### 主干

**there is something … in …**

在…… 中存在某种……

### 细分

- something：主语
    
- truly forbidding：形容词作**后置定语**
    
    = something that is truly forbidding
    
    令人反感、令人难以接受的东西
    
- **in a child taking up her elders**
    
    - a child：逻辑主语
    - **taking up**：现在分词作定语
        
        take up sb：这里是**顶撞、反驳、跟长辈对着干**（旧用法）
    - in that manner：以那种方式、那样做
    

**整部分结构：**

there be + 名词 + 形容词后置 + in + 名词 + 分词短语

## 1. A breakfast-room adjoined the drawing-room, I slipped in there.

- **A breakfast-room**：主语
- **adjoined**：谓语（一般过去时），意为 “毗邻、紧邻”
- **the drawing-room**：宾语
- **I slipped in there**：并列句
    
    - slip in：悄悄溜进去
    - 这里用逗号连接两个独立句，是**文学性松散句**，不算严格语法错误。
    

---

## 2. It contained a bookcase:

- **It**：主语（指代 breakfast-room）
- **contained**：谓语
- **a bookcase**：宾语

---

## 3. I soon possessed myself of a volume,

- **possess oneself of sth**
    
    固定短语：**取得、获取、把…… 拿到手**
- 结构：
    
    I（主） + possessed（谓） + myself（反身宾语） + of a volume（介词短语）

---

- **taking care**：**现在分词作伴随状语**
- **that it should be…**：that 引导**宾语从句**，作 taking care 的宾语
- **should**：表示**刻意、力求**
- **one**：指代 a book
- **stored with pictures**：**过去分词短语作后置定语**
    
    = which was stored with pictures
    
    意为 “装满图画的、带插图的”。

---

## 5. I mounted into the window-seat:

- **mounted**：攀登、爬上（过去式）
- **window-seat**：窗座、靠窗的软垫座位

---

- **gathering up my feet**：
    
    现在分词作**方式 / 伴随状语**：**缩起双脚**
- **sat cross-legged**：
    
    sat（谓语） + cross-legged（**形容词作主语补足语**）

- **like a Turk**：介词短语作**方式状语**

---

- **nearly close**：副词 + 形容词，作**宾语补足语**

---

## 8. I was shrined in double retirement.

- **was shrined**：被动语态，本义 “供奉在神龛里”，这里意为**被环绕、被藏起来**

    - 一是窗帘遮挡，二是缩在窗座里
