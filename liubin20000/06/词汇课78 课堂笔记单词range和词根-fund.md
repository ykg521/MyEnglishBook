## “range”表示“一系列；范围；牧场”等含义。

range [reɪn(d)ʒ] n. (同类事物的) 一系列；范围；(能够到达或探知的) 最大范围；山脉；射击场；大牧场 vi. (在一定幅度内) 变化，四处移动 974

词源：Meaning "scope, extent" first recorded late 15c.; that of "area over which animals seek food" is from 1620s, from the verb. Specific U.S. sense of "series of townships six miles in width" is from 1785. Sense of "distance a gun can send a bullet" is recorded from 1590s; meaning "place used for shooting practice" is from 1862. The cooking appliance so called since mid-15c., for unknown reasons. Originally a stove built into a fireplace with openings on top for multiple operations. Range-finder attested from 1872.
助记：move in a large area：在范围很大的区域内移动 → 范围；一系列；(能够到达或探知的) 最大范围；大牧场；(在一定幅度内)变化；射击场

搭配：in range or within range/out of range：在可触及的范围内/外  

a wide range of：大范围的；许多各种不同的  

range from A to B：范围从 A 到 B

## “fund- = bottom”，含义为“底部，基础”。

fund [fʌnd] vt. 资助 n. (为特定目的而筹集或保留的)专项资金 755

词源：1670s, "a bottom, the bottom; foundation, groundwork," from French fond "a bottom, floor, ground" (12c.), also "a merchant's basic stock or capital," from Latin fundus "bottom, foundation, piece of land," from PIE root *bhudh- "bottom, base" (source also of Sanskrit budhnah, Greek pythmen "foundation, bottom," Old English botm "lowest part"). Meaning "stock of money or wealth available for some purpose" is from 1690s; sense of "store of anything to be drawn upon" is from 1704. Funds "money at one's disposal" is from 1728.
助记：fund(=bottom=base：根基，底)→基金

搭配：fund a research：资助一个研究

raise fund：筹集资金

provident fund：公积金

profound [prə'faʊnd] adj. 深刻的；极大的；知识渊博的

词源：c. 1300, "characterized by intellectual depth," from Old French profund (12c., Modern French profond), from Latin profundus "deep, bottomless, vast," also "obscure; profound; immoderate". The literal and figurative senses both were in Latin, but English, having already deep, employed this word primarily in its figurative sense. Related: Profoundly.
助记：pro-(= forth：向前)+ found(= fund = bottom：底部；基础)→ 底部基础继续向下伸展的，向前伸到底的 → 无底的；基础深的 → 引申词义深刻的；知识渊博的

搭配：profound knowledge：深厚的知识

profound effect：深远的影响

profound disagreement：极大的分歧

写作例句：互联网对我们的沟通方式产生了深远的影响。

The Internet has a profound effect on our way of communication.
