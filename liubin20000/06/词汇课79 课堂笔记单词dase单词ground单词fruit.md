## 词根“funct = work”表示“起作用；有功效；功能，职责”

词源：*bhrūg-, Proto-Indo-European root meaning "to enjoy," with derivatives referring to agricultural products.

It is the hypothetical source of/evidence for its existence is provided by: Latin fruí "to use, enjoy," fructus "an enjoyment, proceeds, fruit, crops;" Old English brucan "use, enjoy, possess," German brauchen "to use."

Sense evolved in Latin from "useful" to "profitable" to "economical." Related: Frugally.
