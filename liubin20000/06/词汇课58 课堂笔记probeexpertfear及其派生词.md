## Probe 含义为调查，搜索，其派生词有 probing, probation, probable, probably, probability 等。

probe [prəʊb] vt. 调查；搜索 vi. 用探针探查 n. 调查；探针 5241

词源：early 15c., "instrument for exploring wounds, etc.," also "an examination," from Medieval

Latin proba "examination," in Late Latin "a test, proof," from Latin probare "show, demonstrate; test, inspect; judge by trial". Meaning "act of probing" is 1890, from the verb; figurative sense of "penetrating investigation" is from 1903. Meaning "small, unmanned exploratory craft" is attested from 1953.
助记：prob-(=prove：证明；检验)→探究；探查(都是为了“证明”是否正确)

搭配：probe for understanding：寻求理解

probe into corruption：调查腐败

a fibre-optic probe：一根光纤探针

probe the underbrush：搜索了低矮灌丛

搭配：on probation：服缓刑

## "fear, peril, panic"分别表示"害怕；危险；惊慌"

fear [fɪə] n. 害怕；担忧；(发生坏事的) 可能性 vt. 害怕；担心 vi. 担心 988
助记：词根为 per-：尝试 → 由尝试引申词义(因为尝试未知事物而带来的)风险，害怕 → 害怕；担忧

搭配：be motivated by fear of failure：因担心失败而被激发  

in fear of：在对…的担心中；在对…的恐惧中  

for fear of：惟恐；以免

例句：She was afraid to say anything to them for fear of hurting their feelings.

她不敢对他们说什么，惟恐伤害他们的感情。
