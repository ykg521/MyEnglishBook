“词根 kwo-/qu-”开头的单词一般表示“who, which, how, why, where, in what respect, to what extent”，即英文中的特殊疑问词，如“what, when, whence, where, whether, which, whither, who, whoever, whom, whose, why, how”等单词都是源于“kwo-/qu-”这个词根。

“query”表示“提问”，“quest”表示“探求；寻求”

query ['kwɪəri] n. 疑问 vt. 对...提出疑问；询问

词源：1530s, quaere "a question," from Latin quaere "ask," imperative of quaerere "to seek, look for; strive, endeavor, strive to gain; ask, require, demand;" figuratively "seek mentally, seek to learn, make inquiry". 来自拉丁语 quaere=a question, ask: 疑问；询问；查问



搭配：query your bill：对账单提出质疑  

have any queries：有任何疑问

quest [kwest] n. (长久而艰难的) 搜寻；探求 vi. 搜寻；探求 4108

词源：c. 1300, "an inquest;" early 14c., "a search for something" (especially of judicial inquiries or hounds seeking game), from Old French queste "search, quest, chase, hunt, pursuit; inquest, inquiry" (12c., Modern French quête), properly "the act of seeking," and directly from Medieval Latin questa "search, inquiry," alteration of Latin quaesitus (fem. quaesita) "sought-out, select," past participle of quaerere "seek, gain, ask".
助记：question 问题(即要去 “寻找” 答题的东西)→ quest 寻求；搜寻

搭配：in quest of something：寻找某物

例句：Job-hunting tasks gain added meaning as you sense their importance in your quest for a more meaningful work life. 找工作能获得更多的意义，因为你在寻求一个更有意义的工作生活时意识到了他们的重要性。

cue [kju:] n. (戏剧中暗示下一位演员接言或出场的)尾白，提示；提示；暗示[+ for]；触发事件 vt. (尤指在表演中)提示 4929

词源：在16和17世纪时，在西方戏剧的脚本上，往往有表示提醒演员说话或上场的提示符号Q，Q是拉丁单词quando(=英语中的when)的缩写。后来，这个符号逐渐演变为单词cue，其发音跟Q一样。词义也逐渐扩展，不仅可以用来表示戏剧中的提示，还可以表示任何“提示”，泛指“暗示”。
