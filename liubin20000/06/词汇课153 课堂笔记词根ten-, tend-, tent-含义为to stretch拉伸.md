词根 ten-, tend-, tent-含义为 to stretch, thin, string, sinew，表示“延伸，拉长”，衍生义为“细的，薄的；一段细绳；肌腱，筋”等含义。

词源：*ten-

Proto-Indo-European root meaning “to stretch,” with derivatives meaning “something stretched, a string; thin.”
