## 词根/单词“sec-, sect, seg-, saw”都有“to cut: 切”的含义。

Proto-Indo-European root meaning "to cut."

It is the hypothetical source of/evidence for its existence is provided by: Hittite shakk-"to know, pay attention to;" Latin secare "to cut," sectio "a cutting, cutting off, division;" Old Church Slavonic seko, sešti "to cut," sečivo "ax, hatchet," Russian seč"to cut to pieces;" Lithuanian jšekti "to engrave, carve;" Albanian šate "mattock;" Old Saxon segasna, Old English sigče "scythe;" Old English secg "sword," seax "knife, short sword;" Old Irish doescim "I cut."

section['sekʃn] vt. 分(成若干部分)n. 部分；剖面图 821

词源：来自古法语 section or directly from Latin sectionem (nominative sectio) "a cutting, cutting off, division", 含义为“切断；剪下；分割；部门”。来自 secure "to cut," 来自词根 sek-"to cut."
助记：sect-(=cut：切割)+ -ion(名词后缀)→ 切割出来的东西 → 分；部分；剖面图

搭配：be peeled or sectioned：被去皮或切分

a vertical section：垂直剖面图  

cross section：横截面

搭配：a sectional building：组合式建筑

sector ['sektə] n. (经济的) 部门，行业；部分；军事管制区 1988
