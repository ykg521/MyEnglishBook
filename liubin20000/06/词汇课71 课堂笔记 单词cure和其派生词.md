## cure 的含义为 “治愈，解决；解决方法”

cure [kjʊə; kjoː] vt. 治愈(疾病、病人)；解决；加工处理 n. (有治愈效果的)药物、疗法；解决方法[+for] 4633

词源：vt. late 14c., "to restore to health or a sound state," from Old French curer and directly from Latin curare "take care of," hence, in medical language, "treat medically, cure" (see cure (n.1)). In reference to fish, pork, etc., "prepare for preservation by drying, salting, etc.," attested by 1743. Related: Cured; curing. n. c. 1300, "care, heed," from Latin cura "care, concern, trouble," with many figurative extensions over time such as "study; administration; office of a parish priest; a mistress," and also "means of healing, successful remedial treatment of a disease, medical care" (late 14c.).

搭配：cure the sufferers：治愈患者

a cure for cancer：一个对癌症治愈方法

incurable [ɪnˈkjʊərəb(ə)l] adj. 无法治愈的；无可救药的 17938
助记：in-(不；非)+ -cur-(treat medically：医疗)+ -able(形容词后缀，能...的)→ 无法进行医疗的 → 无法治愈的；无可救药的

搭配：an incurable romantic：一个无可救药的浪漫主义者  

incurable disease：绝症
