2. back 资助；支持(政府或其他权力团体)

释义：to support a person or plan by providing money or practical help – used about governments or other powerful groups

例句：The £100 million scheme is backed by the British government.

翻译：这个耗资一亿英镑的计划由英国政府资助。

3. endorse 正式赞同；支持

释义：to formally and officially say that you support a person, plan, or idea

翻译：农业部长们拒绝支持该委员会的提议。

## Pore 含义为 “毛孔，气孔”

pore [pɔː] n. (皮肤的)毛孔；(植物表面的)气孔；细孔 vi. 仔细阅读；凝视，注视 11871
助记：来自拉丁语 porus，孔隙。来自希腊语 poros，孔隙，门孔，通道。来自 PIE*per-，向前，通过。

搭配：pore over or through：仔细研究

penetrate every pore: 深入每一个毛孔

小结：-por 的核心含义为 “口，通道”；港口(一个国家进出的 “口”)；大门(一个家庭进出的 “口”)；气孔(气体，水等进出的 “口”)

porous ['pɔːrəs] adj. 多孔的；能渗透的，透水[气]的；多漏洞的；松懈的 14033
助记：pore(=an opening, passage, way：孔；通道)+ -ous(形容词后缀)→ 多孔的 → 能渗透的；透水气的 → 引申义为多漏洞的；松懈的

搭配：porous material：渗透性材料  

the porous border between...：...之间防备松懈的边界线

## Fare 在其所构成单词的单词中，一般包含 passage, journey: 通路；旅行等含义，因此有票价的意思。

fare [feə] n.(公共汽车；火车；飞机等的)票价；vi. 进展 4830

词源：n. Old English fær "journey, road, passage, expedition," from strong neuter of faran "to journey" (see fare (v.)); merged with faru "journey, expedition, companions, baggage," strong fem. of faran. Original sense is obsolete, except in compounds (wayfarer, sea-faring, etc.). Meaning "food provided" is c. 1200 (Old English also had the word in the sense "means of subsistence"); that of "conveyance" appears in Scottish early 15c. and led to sense of "payment for passage" (1510s). Meaning "person conveyed in a vehicle" is from 1560s.

vi. Old English faran "to journey, set forth, go, travel, wander, make one's way," also "be, happen, exist; be in a particular condition".
助记：和 “per-穿过” 有紧密联系 → fare：穿过，走过，通过；远行 → 进一步引申为 “过路费，票价”；以及一件事情的进程，进展

词源：fare 的原始意义是“走”，来源于古印欧语 por-(离去；通过)在日耳曼语中派生的 fer-(走)
助记：fare = payment for passage, to journey, to go one's way: 旅行；进展
