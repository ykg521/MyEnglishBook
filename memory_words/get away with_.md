# get away with


get away with 是一个非常常用的短语动词（phrasal verb），核心意思是：

做了坏事（或违规的事）却没有被发现、没有受到惩罚（to do something wrong or against the rules and not get caught or punished for it）。

它常带有侥幸、逃脱责任的意味，有时也用于较轻的情况（“侥幸成功”或“没出事”）。

同义词（Synonyms）
根据强度和语境，从最常用到较正式排序：

get off（最常用、最自然的替换，尤其是“get off lightly”）
escape punishment / escape the consequences
avoid punishment / dodge the consequences
get away scot-free（完全逃脱惩罚）
beat the rap（美式俚语，逃脱罪责）
slip through the net（从网中溜走）
go unpunished
evade / elude consequences
较口语/俚语：

pull it off（成功做到但有风险）
get off the hook（摆脱麻烦）
反义词：get caught, get punished, face the consequences, be held accountable。

联想词 & 常见搭配（Collocations）
get away with + doing something（最经典结构）
get away with cheating / lying / stealing / murder
get away with it（最常用简短形式）
almost / nearly / somehow get away with
always / usually / never get away with
let someone get away with something（允许某人逃脱惩罚）
think you can get away with something
例句：

He cheated on the exam but got away with it.
She always gets away with being late because she’s the boss’s favorite.
You’ll never get away with lying to your parents forever.
地道表达 & 习语（Natural Expressions）
英语母语者最常用、最地道的说法包括：

Get away with murder（最著名习语！意思是“为所欲为却不受惩罚”，常带夸张或不满）
“His parents let him get away with murder — he never does any chores.”
Get away with it（最口语、最自然）
“I can’t believe he got away with it again!”
Let someone get away with something
“The teacher shouldn’t let students get away with cheating.”
Think you can get away with something
“Did you really think you could get away with skipping class?”
其他生动用法：

“She got away with breaking the rules because no one noticed.”
“The company tried to get away with paying lower taxes, but they got caught.”
“Kids always try to get away with as much as possible when their parents aren’t looking.”
“He got away with only a warning instead of a fine.”
常见场景：

父母/老师： “You’re not getting away with that behavior!”
职场/法律： “He thought he could get away with embezzlement, but the audit caught him.”
发音（Pronunciation）
get away with：/ɡet əˈweɪ wɪð/ （美式）或 /ɡet əˈweɪ wɪð/ （英式）
重音通常在 away（əˈWEY）。
口语中常连读： “getawaywith” 听起来像 “ged-uh-WAY-with”。
过去式：got away with（/ɡɑːt/ 或 /ɡɒt/）。
拼写 & 易混词（Spelling & Confusable Words）
正确拼写：get away with（三个独立的词，作为短语动词）。
常见错误：getawaywith（写成一个词）、get awaywith（漏空格）。
易混词 / 易混短语：

get away with vs get away from
Get away with：逃脱惩罚（做了坏事却没事）。
Get away from：逃离某个地方/人/情况（get away from the city / get away from him）。
get away with vs get off with
Get off with：有时指“以轻罚逃脱”或（英式俚语）与某人亲热。
get away with murder（固定习语，不要改成其他动词）。
中文对应地道说法（参考）
逃脱惩罚、侥幸过关、没被抓到
为所欲为却没事（get away with murder）
做了坏事却逍遥法外
蒙混过关、溜过去
小结使用建议：

最自然表达 → get away with it 和 get away with murder（超级常用）。
想显得地道 → 多用 let someone get away with（允许某人逃脱）或 think you can get away with（你以为你能逃掉？）。
正式场合 → 用 escape punishment 或 go unpunished。
