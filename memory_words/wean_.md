# wean


wean 是一个相对正式且有特定语境的动词，主要含义是：

使断奶、逐渐戒除、使逐渐脱离（to gradually accustom a baby or young animal to food other than its mother’s milk; to make someone gradually stop depending on something）。

它常用于婴儿断奶、戒除习惯（如戒奶嘴、戒烟、戒网瘾）等场景，强调逐步、缓慢的过程。

发音（Pronunciation）
英式 & 美式通用：/wiːn/ （WEEN）
读作 WEEN（长元音 /iː/，像 “queen”、“green” 中的 ee 音）。
一音节，重音在唯一音节上。
同义词（Synonyms）
根据语境不同，同义词有明显区别：

1. 婴儿/动物断奶
wean off breast milk / formula
accustom to solid food
2. 逐渐戒除某种依赖（最常用场景）
gradually stop
break the habit of
detach from
disengage from
withdraw from
phase out（逐步停止）
ease off
较正式：

discontinue gradually
detach oneself from
联想词 & 常见搭配（Collocations）
wean a baby / child / puppy
wean someone off something（最经典搭配）
wean off breast milk
wean off drugs / cigarettes / social media
wean someone from something
gradually / slowly wean
weaning process / weaning period
例句：

It’s time to wean the baby off breast milk.
She is trying to wean herself off caffeine.
The doctor helped him weanoff the medication slowly.
地道表达 & 习语（Natural Expressions）
母语者常用以下自然、生动的说法：

Wean off（最常用、最地道）
“It took several weeks to wean the baby off formula.”
Wean someone from something
“The therapist is helping her wean herself from social media.”
Gradually wean
“You should gradually wean yourself off sleeping pills.”
其他常用表达：

“The puppy is being weaned at eight weeks old.”
“He’s trying to wean himself off junk food.”
“It’s difficult to wean children off their phones these days.”
比喻用法（很常见）：

“Society needs to wean itself off fossil fuels.”
“She is slowly weaning herself from her toxic relationship.”
变形（Word Forms）
动词：wean → weaned → weaning
名词：weaning（断奶过程）
e.g. the weaning process
拼写 & 易混词（Spelling & Confusable Words）
正确拼写：wean（w-e-a-n）
易混词（非常容易出错！）：

wean vs ween（常见拼写错误）
wean vs wine（发音有时接近，但完全不同）
wean vs when（发音接近，尤其快速说话时）
最重要提醒：

不要把 wean 写成 ween（这是最常见的拼写错误）。
正确搭配是 wean someone off / from something，而不是 “wean someone to”。
中文对应地道说法（参考）
断奶（wean a baby）
逐渐戒除（wean off drugs / sugar / phone）
使逐渐脱离（wean from a habit）
断奶期（weaning period）
小结使用建议：

婴儿场景 → wean off breast milk
戒除习惯 → wean oneself off something（最地道）
最常用短语 → wean off 和 gradually wean
