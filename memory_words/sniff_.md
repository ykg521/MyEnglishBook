**音标：** /snɪf/

## 地道表达

### 常用短语

- **sniff at** - 轻蔑地对待
    - *例句：he sniffed at my suggestion as if it were ridiculous.*
- **sniff around** - 四处嗅探
    - *例句：the detective sniffed around for clues.*
- **sniff out** - 发现，嗅出
    - *例句：the dog sniffed out the hidden drugs.*
- **sniff something up** - 吸入某物
    - *例句：don't sniff that powder, it might be dangerous.*

### 习语表达

- **sniff the air** - 嗅空气
    - *例句：the horse sniffed the air nervously.*
- **sniff back tears** - 强忍泪水
    - *例句：she sniffed back tears as she read the letter.*
- **sniff someone/something out** - 追踪发现
    - *例句：reporters sniffed out the scandal quickly.*

## 用法示例

### 动词用法

- **基本用法：** to inhale air through the nose in order to smell something
    - *the dog sniffed the ground for food.*
- **引申用法：** to detect or discover something
    - *investigators sniffed out the corruption.*

### 名词用法

- **基本用法：** an act of inhaling air through the nose
    - *she took a quick sniff of the flower.*
- **引申用法：** a small amount of something inhaled
    - *he had a sniff of the mysterious powder.*

## 常见搭配

### 动词+宾语

- **sniff the air** - 嗅空气
- **sniff a flower** - 闻花香
- **sniff perfume** - 闻香水
- **sniff glue** - 吸胶毒（危险行为）

### 形容词+sniff

- **quick sniff** - 快速嗅闻
- **curious sniff** - 好奇的嗅闻
- **disdainful sniff** - 轻蔑的哼鼻

## 学习提示

1. **注意区分** sniff (嗅) 和 snuff (熄灭)
2. **发音要点**：注意/sn/开头的发音，舌尖轻触上齿龈
3. **使用场合**：sniff多用于日常嗅觉动作，正式场合可用inhale或scent替代
4. **避免误用**：sniff at表示轻蔑，不要与简单的嗅闻混淆

## 扩展知识

### 文化差异

在某些文化中，公开sniffing可能被视为不礼貌，而在其他场合则是正常的行为（如品酒、品茶时）。
