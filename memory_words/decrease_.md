# decrease

# decrease 语言学习指南

## 基本信息

- **单词**: decrease
- **词性**: 动词/名词
- **发音**: /dɪˈkriːs/ (动词), /ˈdiːkriːs/ (名词)
- **释义**: 减少，减小，降低

## 同义词 (Synonyms)

### 动词同义词

- reduce (减少)
- diminish (减小)
- decline (下降)
- dwindle (逐渐减少)
- lower (降低)
- lessen (减轻)
- abate (减弱)
- subside (减退)
- wane (减弱)
- contract (收缩)

### 名词同义词

- reduction (减少)
- decline (下降)
- diminution (减小)
- drop (下降)
- fall (下跌)
- dip (小幅下降)
- slump (暴跌)
- downturn (衰退)

## 联想词 (Collocations)

### 动词搭配

- sharply decrease (急剧减少)
- significantly decrease (显著减少)
- gradually decrease (逐渐减少)
- steadily decrease (稳定减少)
- dramatically decrease (戏剧性减少)
- slightly decrease (略微减少)

### 名词搭配

- population decrease (人口减少)
- price decrease (价格下降)
- temperature decrease (温度降低)
- income decrease (收入减少)
- unemployment decrease (失业率下降)
- crime decrease (犯罪率降低)

### 介词搭配

- decrease in (在...方面的减少)
- decrease by (减少了...)
- decrease to (减少到...)
- decrease from (从...减少)

## 地道表达 (Natural Expressions)

### 常用短语

- **on the decrease**: 在减少中
    - *Crime rates are on the decrease in this city.*
- **a sharp decrease**: 急剧减少
    - *There was a sharp decrease in sales last quarter.*
- **a gradual decrease**: 逐渐减少
    - *We observed a gradual decrease in temperature.*
- **decrease steadily**: 稳定减少
    - *The patient's fever decreased steadily.*

### 实用句型

- **The number of... has decreased significantly**
    - *The number of smokers has decreased significantly in recent years.*
- **There has been a noticeable decrease in...**
    - *There has been a noticeable decrease in air pollution.*
- **... decreased by X%**
    - *Unemployment decreased by 2% last year.*
- **... decreased from X to Y**
    - *The price decreased from *$100 to $*80.*

## 发音易混词 (Pronunciation Confusion)

### 易混单词

- **increase** (/ˈɪŋkriːs/): 增加
    - *发音区别*: "decrease"重音在第二音节，"increase"重音在第一音节
- **decrees** (/dɪˈkriːz/): 法令，敕令
    - *发音区别*: "decrease"结尾是/s/，"decrees"结尾是/z/
- **debris** (/ˈdeɪbriː/): 碎片，残骸
    - *发音区别*: 完全不同的发音模式

## 拼写易混词 (Spelling Confusion)

### 易混单词

- **decease** (/dɪˈsiːs/): 死亡，逝世
    - *区别*: "decease"中间是"ea"，而"decrease"中间是"ea"但结尾是"se"
- **deceit** (/dɪˈsiːt/): 欺骗(古语)
    - *区别*: 拼写和含义完全不同
- **decent** (/ˈdiːsnt/): 体面的，正派的
    - *区别*: 虽然拼写相似，但含义无关

## 变形 (Word Forms)

### 不同词性形式

- **动词原形**: decrease
- **第三人称单数**: decreases
- **现在分词**: decreasing
- **过去式**: decreased
- **过去分词**: decreased
- **名词形式**: decrease
- **形容词形式**: decreasing (递减的)

### 例句展示变形

- **原形**: We need to *decrease* our expenses.
- **第三人称**: The company *decreases* its workforce every winter.
- **现在分词**: *Decreasing* costs is our main priority.
- **过去式**: The temperature *decreased* overnight.
- **过去分词**: Costs have *decreased* significantly.

## 学习提示

### 使用建议

- 注意区分"decrease"和"reduce"的细微差别："decrease"更强调数量或程度的减少，"reduce"更强调人为的缩减
- 记住发音规律：作为动词时重音在第二音节，作为名词时重音在第一音节
- 练习使用"decrease by"和"decrease to"的正确搭配

### 常见错误

- ✘ *The price decreased for *$100 to $*80.*
- ✔ *The price decreased from *$100 to $*80.*
- ✘ *There was a decrease on pollution levels.*
- ✔ *There was a decrease in pollution levels.*
