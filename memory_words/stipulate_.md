# stipulate

## 词义解析

**stipulate** (v.) /ˈstɪpjuleɪt/

**核心含义：** 明确要求或规定；在协议中特别说明

**发音：** /ˈstɪpjuleɪt/ (STIP-yoo-layt)

## 同义词

### 近义词

- **specify** - 明确说明
- **require** - 要求
- **demand** - 需求
- **mandate** - 强制规定
- **dictate** - 规定
- **prescribe** - 规定
- **stipulate** - 特别约定
- **lay down** - 制定（规则）

### 场景化同义表达

- **in legal terms:** provide, state, set forth
- **in business context:** require, demand, insist on
- **in everyday use:** say clearly, make clear

## 联想词

### 相关词汇

- **stipulation** (n.) - 规定，条款
- **stipendiary** (adj.) - 领薪水的
- **stipend** (n.) - 薪水，津贴

### 搭配词汇

- **contractual stipulation** - 合同条款
- **clearly stipulate** - 明确规定
- **stipulate terms** - 规定条款
- **legal stipulation** - 法律规定

## 地道表达

### 常用搭配

1. **stipulate that...** - 规定...
    - *The contract stipulates that payment must be made within 30 days.*
2. **stipulate something** - 明确要求某事
    - *The agreement stipulates a non-disclosure clause.*
3. **as stipulated in...** - 如...中规定
    - *All payments will be made as stipulated in the contract.*
4. **clearly stipulate** - 清楚地规定
    - *The terms must be clearly stipulated to avoid confusion.*

### 实用例句

- *The lease stipulates no pets are allowed in the apartment.*
- *The law stipulates a minimum age for this activity.*
- *We need to stipulate the exact delivery date in the agreement.*
- *As stipulated in our previous meeting, the project will start next Monday.*

## 发音与拼写

### 发音要点

- **音节划分:** stip-u-late (3音节)
- **重音位置:** 第一音节 /ˈstɪp/
- **易错发音:** 注意不要读成"stip-you-let"，正确发音是"stip-yoo-late"

### 拼写易混词

- **stipulate** vs **speculate** - 后者意为"推测"
- **stipulate** vs **stimulate** - 后者意为"刺激"
- **stipulate** vs **stipend** - 后者是"津贴"的意思

### 记忆技巧

- 联想：**STIP**ulate → **ST**ate **I**n **P**articular (特别说明)
- 拆分：stip-u-late → stip(end) + u(late) (联想"规定津贴的支付时间")

## 词形变形

### 动词变形

- **原形:** stipulate
- **第三人称单数:** stipulates
- **现在分词:** stipulating
- **过去式:** stipulated
- **过去分词:** stipulated

### 名词形式

- **stipulation** (n.) - 规定，条款
    - *There is a stipulation about working hours in the contract.*

### 形容词形式

- **stipulated** (adj.) - 规定的，约定的
    - *The stipulated amount must be paid by Friday.*

## 使用场景

### 正式场合

- **法律文件:** *The agreement stipulates that both parties must sign.*
- **合同条款:** *The lease stipulates a security deposit of one month's rent.*
- **官方规定:** *The policy stipulates a maximum of 10 days off per year.*

### 日常对话

- *I stipulate that we eat at my favorite restaurant tonight!*
- *The teacher stipulated that homework must be handed in on time.*

## 常见错误

### 语法错误

- ✘ *The contract stipulate...*
- ✔ *The contract stipulates...* (注意第三人称单数)

### 搭配错误

- ✘ *stipulate for something*
- ✔ *stipulate something* or *stipulate that...*

## 学习小结

**核心记忆：** stipulate = 明确规定/要求

**使用要点：**

1. 多用于正式场合和书面语
2. 常与that从句连用
3. 注意动词变形，特别是第三人称单数
4. 不要与speculate、stimulate等词混淆

**记忆口诀：**
*STIPulate, STIPulate,*
*规定条款不马虎，*
*合同文件常用它，*
*speculate是推测，*
*stimulate是刺激，*
*别把它们搞混啦！*
