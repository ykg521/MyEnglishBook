# characterise 语言学分析

## 基本信息

- **词性**：动词 (verb)
- **英式拼写**：characterise
- **美式拼写**：characterize
- **音标**：/ˈkær.ək.tə.raɪz/

## 同义词 (Synonyms)

### 主要同义词

1. **describe** - 描述
2. **depict** - 描绘
3. **portray** - 刻画
4. **define** - 定义
5. **identify** - 识别
6. **represent** - 代表
7. **illustrate** - 说明
8. **distinguish** - 区分

### 近义词组

- **be characteristic of** - 是...的特征
- **be typical of** - 是...典型的
- **be emblematic of** - 是...的象征

## 联想词 (Collocations)

### 常见搭配

1. **characterise as** - 把...描述为
    - *He characterised the situation as critical.*
2. **characterise by** - 以...为特征
    - *The period is characterised by rapid technological change.*
3. **characterise someone/something** - 描述某人/某物
    - *The author characterised the protagonist as brave but flawed.*

### 学术搭配

- **scientifically characterise** - 科学地描述
- **accurately characterise** - 准确地描述
- **objectively characterise** - 客观地描述

## 地道表达 (Idiomatic Expressions)

### 常用表达方式

1. **be characterised by** - 以...为特征
    - *Modern architecture is characterised by clean lines and minimalism.*
2. **characterise someone as something** - 把某人描述为...
    - *Critics characterised the policy as misguided.*
3. **cannot be characterised as** - 不能被描述为...
    - *This complex issue cannot be characterised as simply black and white.*

### 学术写作表达

- **This study characterises...** - 本研究描述...
- **As characterised by previous research** - 如先前研究所描述的
- **Characterising the phenomenon requires...** - 描述这一现象需要...

## 发音、拼写易混词

### 发音易混词

1. **characterize** /ˈkær.ək.tə.raɪz/ vs **catastrophize** /kəˈtæs.trə.faɪz/
    - 区别：重音位置不同
2. **characterise** vs **categorise** /ˈkæt.ə.ɡə.raɪz/
    - 区别：首音节发音不同

### 拼写易混词

1. **characterise** vs **catastrophise**
    - 区别：前缀不同（char- vs cata-）
2. **characterise** vs **categorise**
    - 区别：中间部分（-ract- vs -tego-）
3. **characterise** vs **caricature**
    - 区别：后缀不同（-ise vs -ure）

### 常见拼写错误

- **× charactorise** (错误：将"character"误拼为"charactor")
- **× characterize** (美式拼写，英式应为"characterise")
- **× charactarise** (错误：漏掉"e")

## 词形变形 (Word Forms)

### 动词变形

- **原形**：characterise
- **第三人称单数**：characterises
- **现在分词**：characterising
- **过去式**：characterised
- **过去分词**：characterised

### 名词形式

- **characterisation** (英式) / **characterization** (美式)
    - *The characterisation of the data was thorough.*

### 形容词形式

- **characteristic**
    - *This is a characteristic feature of the region.*

## 用法对比

### 英式 vs 美式拼写

| 英式拼写 | 美式拼写 | 例句 |
| ------ |------ |------ |
| characterise | characterize | *The report characterises/characterizes the trend accurately.* |
| characterisation | characterization | *The characterisation/characterization was detailed.* |

## 常见错误分析

### 语法错误

1. **错误**：*He characterised the problem complex.*
**正确**：*He characterised the problem as complex.*
    - 需要使用"as"连接补语
2. **错误**：*The era is characterised rapid change.*
**正确**：*The era is characterised by rapid change.*
    - 被动语态需用"by"引出特征

### 搭配错误

- **错误搭配**：characterise about (×)
- **正确搭配**：characterise as (√), characterise by (√)

## 记忆技巧

### 词根分析

- **charact-**：来自"character"（特征）
- **-ise**：动词后缀，表示"使成为..."

### 联想记忆

- **character** (名词) → **characterise** (动词) → **characterisation** (名词)
- 记住"character"是核心词根

## 实用例句

1. **学术写作**：
*The study characterises the phenomenon as multifaceted.*
2. **日常表达**：
*She characterised his behaviour as irresponsible.*
3. **新闻报道**：
*The crisis is characterised by economic instability and social unrest.*
4. **文学描述**：
*The author characterises the protagonist as a tragic hero.*

*注：本文档主要基于英式英语拼写规范（characterise），在美式英语中应使用"characterize"。*
