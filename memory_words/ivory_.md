## 地道表达 (Idiomatic Expressions)

1. **ivory tower**
    - *meaning*: a state of isolation from the practical realities of life, especially in academia
    - *example*: "Academics sometimes live in an ivory tower, disconnected from real-world problems."
2. **ivory trade**
    - *meaning*: the commercial buying and selling of ivory products
    - *example*: "Many countries have banned the ivory trade to protect elephants."
3. **ivory carving tradition**
    - *meaning*: the artistic tradition of carving intricate designs from ivory
    - *example*: "China has a long history of ivory carving tradition."
