# toil 语言学分析文档

## 基本信息

**单词：** toil
**词性：** 名词、动词
**音标：** /tɔɪl/
**中文释义：** 辛苦的劳动；艰难地工作

## 同义词

### 名词同义词（表示辛苦的劳动）

- labor (美式拼写) / labour (英式拼写)
- drudgery
- grind
- slog
- exertion
- toils (复数形式)
- backbreaking work

### 动词同义词（表示艰难地工作）

- labor
- struggle
- slog
- grind
- work hard
- slave away
- sweat

## 联想词

### 相关概念词汇

- **effort** - 努力
- **hardship** - 艰难
- **exhaustion** - 疲惫
- **fatigue** - 疲劳
- **perspiration** - 汗水
- **endeavor** - 努力尝试
- **undertaking** - 事业

### 搭配词汇

- **backbreaking toil** - 累断腰的辛苦劳动
- **arduous toil** - 艰巨的劳动
- **relentless toil** - 不懈的努力
- **years of toil** - 多年的辛苦劳动

## 地道表达

### 常用短语

1. **toil away** - 辛勤工作
    - *They toiled away in the fields all day.*
2. **toil and trouble** - 辛苦与麻烦（源自莎士比亚作品）
    - *Fair is foul, and foul is fair: Hover through the fog and filthy air.*
3. **back-breaking toil** - 累断腰的辛苦劳动
    - *The construction workers endured back-breaking toil.*
4. **toil from dawn till dusk** - 从早到晚辛苦工作
    - *Farmers often toil from dawn till dusk.*

### 习语表达

- **the fruits of one's toil** - 辛勤劳动的成果
    - *After years of toil, she finally achieved success.*
- **be in the toils of** - 陷入困境
    - *The company was in the toils of financial difficulties.*

## 发音、拼写易混词

### 发音相似词

- **toile** /tɔɪl/ - (法语借词) 厚棉布
- **toile** /tɔɪl/ - (建筑) 卫生间（英式英语）
- **toile** /tɔɪl/ - (艺术) 初稿

### 拼写相似词

- **toile** - 厚棉布
- **toile** - 卫生间（英式拼写）
- **toile** - 初稿
- **toile** - 壁毯

### 易混辨析

**toil vs. toile**

- **toil** (t-o-i-l): 辛苦劳动
- **toile** (t-o-i-l-e): 法语借词，指厚棉布或卫生间

**发音区别：**

- toil: /tɔɪl/
- toile: /tɔɪl/ (发音相同，但拼写多一个e)

## 变形

### 词形变化

- **原形：** toil
- **第三人称单数：** toils
- **现在分词：** toiling
- **过去式：** toiled
- **过去分词：** toiled

### 派生词

- **toiler** (n.) - 辛勤工作的人
    - *He was a tireless toiler who never complained.*
- **toilsome** (adj.) - 辛苦的，费力的
    - *The journey was long and toilsome.*

### 复数形式

- **toils** (n.) - 困境，罗网
    - *caught in the toils of debt* (陷入债务困境)

## 用法示例

### 名词用法

1. *After years of toil, the project was finally completed.*
2. *The workers' toil was rewarded with fair wages.*
3. *She escaped the toils of poverty through education.*

### 动词用法

1. *They toiled in the mines for twelve hours a day.*
2. *He toiled away at his novel for ten years.*
3. *The team toiled to meet the deadline.*

## 文化背景

"Toil" 常与"trouble"搭配使用，源自莎士比亚的《麦克白》中女巫的台词："Double, double toil and trouble"，这使得这个词带有一定的文学色彩和历史厚重感。
