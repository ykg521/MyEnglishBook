# cram
cram 的主要意思有三个（最常见的是学习相关）：

塞满、挤满（stuff/pack things tightly into a space）
突击学习 / 临时抱佛脚（study intensively at the last minute before an exam）
狼吞虎咽（eat greedily）

同义词（Synonyms）
1. 塞满 / 挤压（物理意义）

stuff（最常用）
pack
jam
ram
squeeze
force / shove
crowd / overcrowd
compress / compact
chock / wedge
fill to overflowing / chock-full

例句：
The suitcase was crammed with clothes.
= The suitcase was stuffed/jammed/packed with clothes.
2. 突击学习（最常见的学习场景）

bone up (on)（美式，非正式）
swot（英式，非正式，常指死记硬背）
mug up（英式俚语）
grind / hit the books
revise（英式，更中性，指复习）
review（美式）
study hastily / cram for an exam

例句：
I have to cram for tomorrow's exam.
= I have to bone up / swot / mug up / grind for tomorrow's exam.
3. 狼吞虎咽

gorge (oneself)
stuff (your face)
wolf (down)
devour / gobble

联想词 & 搭配（Collocations）

cram for an exam / a test / finals
last-minute cramming
cram session（突击复习时段）
cram school（补习班，亚洲常见）
cram it all in（把所有东西都塞进去 / 学进去）
crammed with（塞满了…）
crammed full of（装得满满的）

地道表达 & 相关习语（Natural Expressions & Idioms）
以下是英语母语者真正会用的表达（尤其是学生党）：

Hit the books — 开始认真学习
“Finals are next week, time to hit the books.”
Burn the midnight oil — 开夜车、熬夜学习
“I’ve been burning the midnight oil trying to prepare for this test.”
Pull an all-nighter — 通宵学习/熬通宵
“I had to pull an all-nighter before the chemistry exam.”
Cram like there’s no tomorrow — 拼命突击
“She’s been cramming like there’s no tomorrow for her SATs.”
Last-minute cram / Cramming at the eleventh hour — 临时抱佛脚
“I always end up cramming at the last minute and then forget everything.”
Brain dump（俚语）——把脑子里临时背的东西全倒出来（考试时常用）
Learn by rote / Rote memorization — 死记硬背（常和 cram 一起被批评）
Pull a cram（不太正式）——临时抱佛脚

其他生动说法：

“I’m going to lock myself in the library and force-feed my brain all this material tonight.”
“No more procrastination — it’s crunch time / do or die time.”
