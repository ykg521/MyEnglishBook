# rivet

## 基本释义

**rivet** (名词/动词) /ˈrɪvɪt/

- **名词**: 铆钉
- **动词**: (使)着迷，(使)全神贯注；用铆钉固定

## 地道表达

### 常用搭配

- **rivet something to/onto something** - 用铆钉固定某物
    - *they riveted the metal plates to the frame*
- **rivet someone's attention** - 吸引某人的注意力
    - *the movie riveted the audience's attention*
- **rivet eyes on something** - 目不转睛地盯着某物
    - *she riveted her eyes on the prize*

### 习语和短语

- **rivet together** - 用铆钉连接在一起
    - *the two pieces were riveted together*
- **rivet in place** - 铆固在原位
    - *the beam was riveted in place*
- **rivet down** - 铆紧，固定住
    - *make sure the cover is riveted down properly*

## 发音、拼写易混词

### 发音相似词

- **river** /ˈrɪvər/ - 河流
- **rival** /ˈraɪvəl/ - 竞争对手
- **revive** /rɪˈvaɪv/ - 复活，恢复

### 拼写相似词

- **revet** - 护坡，加固（较少用）
- **rivet** vs **river** - 注意结尾是"et"不是"er"
- **rivet** vs **rival** - 注意中间字母不同

### 常见拼写错误

- **错误**: rivot, rivitt, riveet
- **正确**: rivet

## 变形

### 词形变化

- **原形**: rivet
- **第三人称单数**: rivets
- **现在分词**: riveting
- **过去式**: riveted
- **过去分词**: riveted

### 派生词

- **riveting** (名词) - 铆接工艺
- **riveter** (名词) - 铆工，铆钉机
- **riveted** (形容词) - 全神贯注的，被吸引的

### 用法示例

**名词用法**:

- *the worker used a rivet to join the two metal sheets*
- *this old bridge was constructed with thousands of rivets*

**动词用法**:

- *the exciting plot riveted the readers from the first page*
- *he was riveted to his seat during the thrilling movie*
- *workers riveted the steel beams together to form the framework*

## 记忆技巧

- 联想"river"（河流），铆钉像河流中的小石子一样连接两岸
- 动词用法联想"fix it"（固定它），铆钉的作用就是固定物体
- "rivet"发音与"river"相似，但结尾是"et"表示"小东西"（铆钉是小物件）
