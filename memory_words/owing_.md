# owing

owing 主要有两个常见用法：

owing to（介词短语）= because of / due to（因为、由于），正式程度较高，常用于书面语或较正式的口语。
owing（形容词）= unpaid / due（未付的、欠着的），多用于财务语境（如 bills owing）。

发音：
/ˈəʊ.ɪŋ/（英式）或 /ˈoʊ.ɪŋ/（美式）—— 读作 “OH-ing” 或 “O-ing”，重音在第一音节。
1. 同义词（Synonyms）
owing to（因为、由于）的同义词（最常用在前）：

because of（最自然、最常用）
due to（最接近，常可互换）
on account of（因为）
as a result of
thanks to（多用于积极原因）
in view of / given（鉴于）
by reason of（较正式）
through（因为）

owing（欠着的、未付的）的同义词：

unpaid
outstanding
due
overdue
owed
payable

例句对比：

The flight was cancelled owing to bad weather.
→ The flight was cancelled because of / due to / on account of bad weather.

2. 联想词 & 相关概念（Associations & Related Words）
owing to 相关：

reason、cause、factor
cancellation、delay、postponement、closure（常与负面事件连用）
owing to circumstances / the fact that... / heavy rain / illness / technical problems

owing（欠款）相关：

debt、bill、payment、amount、balance
owing money / rent / taxes / interest
settle / pay off what is owing

其他关联：

owe（动词：欠、归功于）→ owing 是其现在分词/形容词形式
正式 vs 口语：owing to 比 because of 更正式

3. 地道表达 & 实用短语（Natural Collocations & Idiomatic Expressions）
owing to 的最常见搭配：

owing to bad weather / illness / circumstances / lack of interest / technical difficulties
owing to the fact that...（因为……的事实）
largely / partly owing to...

超级地道表达（推荐使用）：

“The match was postponed owing to heavy rain.”
“Owing to unforeseen circumstances, the event has been cancelled.”
“She was unable to attend owing to family commitments.”
“The delay was owing to a technical fault.”
“Owing to the fact that prices have risen sharply, we need to review the budget.”
“The success of the project was largely owing to her hard work.”

owing（欠款）的地道表达：

“There is still $500 owing on the invoice.”
“How much is owing in total?”
“Please pay the amount owing by the end of the month.”
“Several bills are still owing.”

小贴士：

owing to 在句首或句中都很自然，而 due to 传统上更常用于 be 动词之后（The delay is due to...），但现代英语中两者已高度可互换。
日常口语中人们更常用 because of；owing to 听起来更正式或书面化。
owing to the fact that 稍显啰嗦，正式写作中可简化为 owing to + 名词短语。

4. 发音、拼写易混词（Common Confusions）

owing 与 owning（拥有）发音接近，但拼写和含义不同。
owing 与 owing to 常被视为一个固定短语，不要把 owing 单独当作原因连接词使用。
无特别明显的同音异义词，但注意不要与 owing（欠的）和 owing to（因为）混淆使用场景。
常见拼写错误：把 owing to 写成 owning to 或 owingtoo。

记忆技巧：

Owing to = “归因于” → 像“欠”（owe）某个原因。
与 due to 对比：due to 像“到期应归因于”；owing to 更强调“因为……而导致”。
