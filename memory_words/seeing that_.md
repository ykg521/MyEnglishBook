# seeing that
seeing that（也常写成 seeing as）的主要含义是“既然……”、“考虑到……”、“鉴于……”，用于引出已知的事实或理由，作为后面结论的依据。它属于原因状语从句引导词，语气比 because 更温和、客观，常用于书面语或正式口语。
1. 同义词（Synonyms）
按正式程度和使用频率排序：
最常用、最地道的替换（强烈推荐）：

since（最自然、最万能）
as（简洁，口语书面都行）
because（最直接，但语气稍强）

类似正式程度：

given that（最正式，学术/商务常用）
considering that（考虑到）
in view of the fact that（鉴于……的事实）
inasmuch as（较正式、古雅）
now that（既然现在……，强调时间变化）

较口语/非正式：

seeing as（与 seeing that 几乎完全可互换，更口语化）
being that（美式口语）
for（古旧或正式）
in that（因为，在于）

其他弱同义词：after all、on account of、due to the fact that（较啰嗦，避免过多使用）。
例句对比：

Seeing that it was raining, we stayed indoors.
→ Since it was raining, we stayed indoors.（最自然）
→ Given that it was raining, we stayed indoors.（更正式）
→ Considering that it was raining, we stayed indoors.

注意：seeing as 在美式和英式英语中都比 seeing that 更常见、更随意。
2. 联想词 & 相关概念（Associations & Related Words）
seeing that 常出现在原因/条件/让步语境中，关联以下表达：

原因类引导词：because, since, as, for, now that, in that, seeing as
条件/假设类：given that, provided that, assuming that, supposing that
对比/让步类：whereas, while, although, even though
正式书面变体：in view of、on the grounds that、in light of the fact that

相关短语结构：

Seeing that + 主语 + 动词（完整从句）
Seeing as how + 从句（更口语：Seeing as how you’re here...）
Given the fact that / Considering the fact that

在学术、法律、商务英语中，given that 和 seeing that 经常一起出现，用于论证或政策说明。
3. 地道表达 & 实用短语（Natural Collocations & Idiomatic Expressions）
常见搭配：

Seeing that / Seeing as + 事实 → 结论
Given that + 事实 → 建议/结果
Now that + 变化的事实 → 新行动（强调“现在既然……”）

超级地道的日常表达（推荐替换 seeing that 时使用）：

“Since you’re already here, you might as well help.”
“Given that prices are rising, we should cut costs.”
“Considering it’s your first time, you did really well.”
“As it’s getting late, let’s wrap this up.”
“Now that the meeting is over, we can finally relax.”

更自然的口语版本：

“You’re tired? Makes sense we should head home.”（间接表达原因）
“With the traffic being so bad, it’ll take forever.”
“The way things are, we have no choice but to wait.”

正式/书面地道表达（报告、论文、邮件）：

“Given that the data shows a clear trend, we recommend…”
“In view of the fact that sales have dropped, a review is necessary.”
“In light of recent developments, the policy will be updated.”

习语/扩展表达（间接表达“鉴于”含义）：

All things considered（综合考虑）
Under the circumstances（在这种情况下）
Taking everything into account（把一切都考虑进去）
That being the case（既然如此）
Be that as it may（尽管如此，但……）

小贴士：

日常对话中，since 和 as 最自然，几乎不会出错。
学术写作中优先用 given that 或 since，避免重复使用 seeing that（听起来稍显老派）。
seeing as how 是美式口语加强版，常用于解释明显的事实。

例句实战：

Seeing that nobody objected, the motion was passed.
→ Since nobody objected, the motion was passed.（更流畅）
