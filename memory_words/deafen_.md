# deafen 词汇学习文档

## 基本信息

**单词：** deafen
**词性：** 动词 (verb)
**音标：** /ˈdef.ən/
deafen sb. to make somebody unable to hear, either permanently or for a short time

## 同义词 (Synonyms)

### 近义词

- **muffle** - 使声音减弱
- **dull** - 使迟钝
- **stun** - 使震惊
- **numb** - 使麻木
- **overwhelm** - 使不知所措
- **assault** - 袭击（感官）

### 精准替换

- **make deaf** - 使变聋
- **impair hearing** - 损害听力
- **deprive of hearing** - 剥夺听力

## 反义词 (Antonyms)

- **awaken** - 唤醒
- **alert** - 使警觉
- **sensitize** - 使敏感
- **enhance hearing** - 增强听力

## 联想词 (Collocations)

### 常见搭配

- **deafen someone** - 使某人变聋
- **deafen the audience** - 使观众耳聋
- **deafen with noise** - 用噪音震耳欲聋
- **deafen by sound** - 被声音震聋

### 修饰语搭配

- **completely deafen** - 完全震聋
- **temporarily deafen** - 暂时震聋
- **permanently deafen** - 永久震聋
- **almost deafen** - 几乎震聋

## 地道表达 (Natural Expressions)

### 常用短语

- **be deafened by** - 被...震得耳聋
    - *The audience was deafened by the explosion.*
- **deafen someone to** - 使某人对...充耳不闻
    - *The constant noise deafened him to the beauty of music.*
- **deafen oneself** - 使自己耳聋
    - *Don't deafen yourself with loud music.*

### 习语表达

- **deafening silence** - 令人不安的沉默
    - *There was a deafening silence after his announcement.*
- **turn a deaf ear** - 充耳不闻
    - *The government turned a deaf ear to the protests.*

## 发音易混词 (Homophones)

- **defen** - 无此词（易与defend混淆）
- **deffin** - 无此词（拼写错误）

## 拼写易混词 (Common Misspellings)

- **defean** - 错误拼写（正确：deafen）
- **deffin** - 错误拼写
- **defin** - 错误拼写（与define混淆）

## 词形变形 (Word Forms)

### 动词变形

- **原形：** deafen
- **第三人称单数：** deafens
- **现在分词：** deafening
- **过去式：** deafened
- **过去分词：** deafened

### 相关词性转换

- **名词：** deafness（耳聋的状态）
- **形容词：** deaf（聋的）
- **副词：** deafly（聋地，不常用）

## 派生词 (Derivatives)

- **deaf** (adj.) - 聋的
- **deafness** (n.) - 耳聋
- **deafening** (adj.) - 震耳欲聋的
- **deafened** (adj.) - 被震聋的

## 用法示例 (Usage Examples)

1. *The loud music at the concert almost ****deafened**** me.*
2. *The explosion was so powerful it could ****deafen**** people nearby.*
3. *Constant exposure to loud noise can ****deafen**** you over time.*
4. *The ****deafening**** roar of the jet engines made conversation impossible.*

## 语义辨析 (Semantic Differences)

- **deafen** 强调声音大到损害听力
- **muffle** 强调声音被减弱或阻隔
- **dull** 强调感觉变得迟钝
- **stun** 强调突然的震惊效果

## 学习提示 (Learning Tips)

1. 记住  **"deaf"**  是核心词根，表示"聋"
2. 后缀  **"-en"**  表示"使成为"，如 weaken, strengthen
3. 注意与  **"defend"**  区分，发音和拼写都不同
4. 常用于被动语态：**be deafened by**
