# flank

# flank 语言学习指南

## 基本信息

- **单词**: flank
- **词性**: 名词、动词
- **音标**: /flæŋk/

## 同义词 (Synonyms)

### 名词同义词

- side (侧面)
- edge (边缘)
- border (边界)
- margin (边缘)
- periphery (外围)

### 动词同义词

- surround (包围)
- encircle (环绕)
- border (毗邻)
- adjoin (邻接)
- abut (接界)

## 联想词 (Collocations)

### 名词搭配

- **left/right flank** (左/右翼)
- **enemy flank** (敌军侧翼)
- **open flank** (暴露的侧翼)
- **strong flank** (坚固的侧翼)
- **weak flank** (薄弱的侧翼)

### 动词搭配

- **attack the flank** (攻击侧翼)
- **protect the flank** (保护侧翼)
- **expose the flank** (暴露侧翼)
- **turn the flank** (包抄侧翼)
- **hold the flank** (守住侧翼)

## 地道表达 (Idiomatic Expressions)

### 军事用语

- **outflank the enemy** - 包抄敌人
    - "We need to outflank the enemy to win this battle."
- **flank attack** - 侧翼攻击
    - "The general ordered a flank attack on the enemy position."

### 日常用语

- **flank steak** - 牛 flank steak (牛腹肉/ flank 牛排)
    - "I ordered a delicious flank steak at the restaurant."
- **on both flanks** - 在两侧
    - "There were guards positioned on both flanks."

## 发音、拼写易混词

### 发音相似词

- **blank** /blæŋk/ - 空白的
- **clank** /klæŋk/ - 金属碰撞声
- **shrank** /ʃræŋk/ - 缩小(过去式)

### 拼写易混词

- **flan** - 一种甜点
- **flank** - 侧翼，肋部
- **flunk** - 失败，不及格
- **blank** - 空白的

### 常见拼写错误

- **flank** (正确) vs **flanck** (错误)
- **flank** (正确) vs **flaunk** (错误)

## 变形 (Word Forms)

### 不同词性形式

- **名词**: flank (单数), flanks (复数)
- **动词**: flank (原形), flanks (第三人称单数), flanked (过去式), flanked (过去分词), flanking (现在分词)

### 词形变化示例

- **名词用法**:
    - "The castle has strong defenses on both flanks."
    - "The soldier protected the right flank."
- **动词用法**:
    - "The two towers flank the main entrance."
    - "Trees flank the path on both sides."
    - "The army flanked the enemy position."

## 用法示例

### 军事场景

- "The commander decided to attack the enemy's left flank."
- "Our troops successfully flanked the enemy and won the battle."

### 地理描述

- "The mountains flank the valley on both sides."
- "A row of trees flanks the driveway."

### 食物相关

- "Flank steak is a popular cut of beef for grilling."
- "I marinated the flank steak for several hours before cooking."

## 学习提示

### 记忆技巧

- 联想"flat"和"bank"，想象平坦的河岸两侧
- 记住"flank steak"这个常用食物词汇

### 使用场景

- 描述物体的位置关系
- 军事战术讨论
- 食物烹饪
- 体育比赛中的位置描述

### 常见错误

- 误用为"flunk"表示失败
- 混淆"flank"和"flan"的拼写
- 忘记动词变位规则
