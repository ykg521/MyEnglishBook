# stifle 学习笔记

## 基本信息

- **单词**: stifle
- **词性**: 动词 (vt.)
- **发音**: /ˈstaɪfəl/

## 同义词 (Synonyms)

### 主要同义词

- suppress - 抑制，镇压
- smother - 窒息，扼杀
- suffocate - 使窒息
- repress - 压抑，压制
- quell - 平息，镇压
- curb - 控制，抑制
- restrain - 抑制，限制
- inhibit - 抑制，阻碍
- throttle - 勒死，扼杀
- muzzle - 塞住嘴，抑制

### 场景同义词

**抑制情绪时**:

- restrain, control, hold back, keep in

**阻止发展时**:

- hinder, impede, obstruct, block

## 联想词 (Collocations)

### 动宾搭配

- stifle creativity - 抑制创造力
- stifle innovation - 扼杀创新
- stifle growth - 阻碍发展
- stifle freedom - 限制自由
- stifle expression - 抑制表达
- stifle laughter - 忍住笑声
- stifle a yawn - 强忍哈欠
- stifle dissent - 压制异议

### 副词修饰

- completely stifle - 完全扼杀
- effectively stifle - 有效抑制
- deliberately stifle - 故意压制
- gradually stifle - 逐渐扼杀

## 地道表达 (Natural Expressions)

### 常用短语

- **stifle someone's spirit** - 扼杀某人的精神
    - *Don't let anyone stifle your creative spirit.*
- **stifle a laugh** - 忍住笑
    - *I had to stifle a laugh during the serious meeting.*
- **stifle innovation** - 抑制创新
    - *Bureaucracy often stifles innovation in large companies.*
- **be stifled by** - 被...压抑
    - *Artistic expression was stifled by the oppressive regime.*

### 例句展示

1. *Tight regulations can stifle small businesses.*
繁琐的法规可能会扼杀小企业。
2. *She tried to stifle her sobs with a handkerchief.*
她试图用手帕抑制住自己的抽泣。
3. *The government was accused of trying to stifle free speech.*
政府被指控试图压制言论自由。

## 发音、拼写易混词 (Common Confusions)

### 发音相似词

- **style** /staɪl/ - 风格
- **still** /stɪl/ - 仍然
- **steal** /stiːl/ - 偷窃
- **stall** /stɔːl/ - 摊位

### 拼写相似词

- **strife** - 冲突，斗争
- **strive** - 努力，奋斗
- **stifle** - 抑制，扼杀
- **stifle** 与 **strive** 容易混淆

### 易混辨析

**stifle vs. strive**

- *stifle* (抑制) - 使停止或减弱
- *strive* (努力) - 积极争取

**stifle vs. style**

- *stifle* 发音为 /ˈstaɪfəl/
- *style* 发音为 /staɪl/
- 注意尾音的差别

## 变形 (Word Forms)

### 不同时态

- **原形**: stifle
- **第三人称单数**: stifles
- **过去式**: stifled
- **过去分词**: stifled
- **现在分词**: stifling

### 派生词

- **stifling** (adj.) - 令人窒息的
    - *The room was stifling hot.*
- **stifled** (adj.) - 被抑制的
    - *a stifled cry* - 被压抑的哭声
- **stiflingly** (adv.) - 令人窒息地
    - *The rules were stiflingly strict.*

## 使用注意事项

### 正式程度

- **stifle** 属于中性偏正式用语
- 适用于书面语和正式场合
- 口语中可用 "hold back", "keep in" 替代

### 搭配习惯

- 多与抽象名词搭配 (creativity, innovation, freedom)
- 也可与具体动作搭配 (laugh, yawn, sob)
- 通常用于负面语境，表示不好的抑制

### 常见错误

**错误**: *He tried to stifle his happiness.*
**正确**: *He tried to stifle his laughter.*

**错误**: *The rules stifle easily.*
**正确**: *The rules are easily stifled.* 或 *The rules stifle creativity easily.*

## 同根词扩展

### 词根分析

- 词根 "stif-" 表示 "press" 或 "suppress"
- 后缀 "-le" 表示反复动作

### 相关词汇

- **stuff** - 填充，塞满 (同源)
- **stout** - 坚固的，肥胖的 (同源)
- **stew** - 炖，焖 (同源概念)
