# quench 单词详解

## 基本信息

**单词：** quench
**音标：** /kwentʃ/
**词性：** 动词

---

## 同义词 (Synonyms)

### 核心同义词

- **extinguish** - 熄灭（尤指火或光）
- **satisfy** - 满足（需求、欲望）
- **suppress** - 压制，抑制
- **douse** - 浸入，浇灭
- **slake** - 解（渴），满足

### 场景化同义词

**解渴场景：**

- appease
- assuage
- allay

**灭火场景：**

- put out
- douse
- smother

### 语义联想

---

## 地道表达 (Collocations)

### 常用搭配

1. **quench your thirst** - 解你的渴
    - "After the run, I needed to quench my thirst."
2. **quench a fire** - 灭火
    - "The firefighters worked hard to quench the blaze."
3. **quench someone's desire/curiosity** - 满足某人的欲望/好奇心
    - "The documentary quenched my curiosity about space."
4. **quench the flames** - 扑灭火焰
    - "They used sand to quench the flames."

### 习语表达

- **quench the light** - 熄灭灯光（文学用法）
- **quench one's appetite** - 满足食欲

## 发音、拼写易混词

### 发音相似词

- **quaint** /kweɪnt/ - 古雅的，别致的
- **queue** /kjuː/ - 队列
- **quick** /kwɪk/ - 快速的

### 拼写相似词

- **quaint** - 注意结尾是"nt"不是"ch"
- **quiver** - 颤抖
- **quiet** - 安静的

### 易混淆点

- 发音时注意"quench"中的"ch"发/tʃ/音，不同于"quaint"中的"nt"
- 拼写上容易与"quaint"混淆，注意"quench"包含"ch"组合

---

## 单词变形 (Word Forms)

### 动词变位

- **原形：** quench
- **第三人称单数：** quenches
- **现在分词：** quenching
- **过去式：** quenched
- **过去分词：** quenched

### 派生词

- **quenchable** (形容词) - 可熄灭的，可抑制的
- **unquenchable** (形容词) - 无法抑制的，不灭的
    - "an unquenchable thirst for knowledge" - 对知识永不满足的渴望

## 用法示例

### 日常用法

1. "I drank a glass of water to quench my thirst."
2. "Nothing could quench her desire to travel the world."

### 文学用法

1. "The rain came to quench the summer drought."
2. "His words failed to quench the growing unrest."

### 技术用法

1. "The metal was quenched in oil to harden it."
2. "The chemical reaction was quenched by adding water."

## 学习提示

### 记忆技巧

- 联想"quick + bench"，想象快速跑到长椅旁解渴
- 注意与"quaint"区分：quench有"ch"音，像喝水的"哧"声

### 常见错误

- ✘ 错误：quench thirst with fire（逻辑错误）
- ✔ 正确：quench fire with water
