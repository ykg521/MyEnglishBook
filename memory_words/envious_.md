# envious
# envious 语言学习资料

## 基本信息

**单词：** envious
**词性：** 形容词
**音标：** /ˈenviəs/
**中文释义：** 羡慕的，嫉妒的

## 同义词（Synonyms）

### 核心同义词

- **jealous** - 嫉妒的，羡慕的
- **covetous** - 贪婪的，觊觎的
- **green-eyed** - 嫉妒的（源自莎士比亚的"green-eyed monster"）
- **possessive** - 占有欲的
- **wistful** - 渴望的，羡慕的

### 近义表达

- **longing** - 渴望的
- **desirous** - 渴望的
- **yearning** - 渴望的
- **aspiring** - 有抱负的
- **ambitious** - 有野心的

## 联想词（Collocations & Associations）

### 常见搭配

- **envious of someone** - 羡慕某人
- **envious of something** - 羡慕某物
- **be envious of success** - 羡慕成功
- **be envious of wealth** - 羡慕财富
- **be envious of beauty** - 羡慕美貌

### 情感相关词

- **envy** (名词) - 嫉妒，羡慕
- **jealousy** (名词) - 嫉妒
- **admiration** - 钦佩
- **resentment** - 怨恨
- **covetousness** - 贪婪

### 反义词

- **content** - 满足的
- **satisfied** - 满意的
- **grateful** - 感激的
- **unenvious** - 不羡慕的

## 地道表达（Idiomatic Expressions）

### 常用表达

- **green with envy** - 嫉妒得发狂
    - *She was green with envy when she saw her friend's new car.*
- **die of envy** - 嫉妒得要死（夸张表达）
    - *I'll die of envy if you go to that concert without me!*
- **be envious of someone's good fortune** - 羡慕某人的好运
    - *I'm envious of your good fortune in finding such a great job.*

### 文学表达

- **the envious eye** - 嫉妒的目光
- **envious fate** - 嫉妒的命运（文学用法）
- **envious tongues** - 嫉妒的流言

## 发音、拼写易混词

### 发音易混词

- **envy** /ˈenvi/ - 动词"羡慕"
- **enviable** /ˈenviəbəl/ - 令人羡慕的
- **envelopment** /ɪnˈvɛləpmənt/ - 包裹，包围
- **environment** /ɪnˈvaɪərənmənt/ - 环境

### 拼写易混词

- **enquiry** (英式拼写，美式为inquiry) - 询问
- **envelop** - 包裹
- **envelope** - 信封
- **enunciate** - 发音清晰
- **enumerate** - 列举

### 常见拼写错误

- **envius** (错误)
- **envious** (正确)
- **envyous** (错误)

## 词形变形（Word Forms）

### 原形

- **envious** - 形容词原级

### 比较级和最高级

- **more envious** - 比较级
- **most envious** - 最高级
    - *She became more envious as her friend's success grew.*
    - *He was the most envious person I've ever met.*

### 相关词形

- **envy** - 名词/动词原形
    - *Don't envy others their success.*
    - *She felt envy when she saw the prize.*
- **envied** - 过去式/过去分词
    - *He envied his brother's talent.*
    - *She was much envied for her beauty.*
- **envying** - 现在分词/动名词
    - *Stop envying what others have.*
    - *Constant envying will make you unhappy.*
- **enviable** - 形容词
    - *She has an enviable collection of art.*
    - *It's an enviable position to be in.*
- **enviably** - 副词
    - *She succeeded enviably in her career.*

## 使用注意事项

### 正式程度

- **envious** 比 **jealous** 稍微正式一些
- 在文学作品中更常见

### 情感色彩

- 可以表示正面的"羡慕"或负面的"嫉妒"
- 具体含义需要根据上下文判断

### 常见错误

- ✘ **envious for** something
- ✔ **envious of** something
- ✘ **envious to** something
- ✔ **envious of** something

## 例句展示

### 正面羡慕

- *I'm envious of your travel experiences.*
- *She was envious of her friend's wonderful vacation.*

### 负面嫉妒

- *He was envious of his colleague's promotion.*
- *The envious neighbor spread rumors about the successful family.*

### 文学用法

- *"The envious moon paled in comparison to her beauty."*
- *"His envious nature made him unpopular among his peers."*
