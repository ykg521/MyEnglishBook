与 sign 同样表达符号、标志含义的单词还有 seal, symbol 及其派生词以及词根 ves-等。单词 seal 表示 “印章”，与单词 sign “标记，记号” 词源相同。

seal [siːl] vt. 确定；封(信封)；封(容器、开口)；封锁(某地区)n. 印章；(容器、开口等)封了的部分；海豹 4062

词源："design stamped on wax," especially one attached to a document as evidence of authenticity, c. 1200, from Old French seel "seal on a letter", from Latin sigillum "small picture, engraved figure, seal," diminutive of signum "identifying mark, sign".

搭配：seal a deal：达成一笔交易  

seal the envelope：封好信封  

seal the border：封锁边境  

note paper bearing the presidential seal：盖有总统印章的便条纸
