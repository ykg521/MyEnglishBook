## 词根-ply，-ple 表示“充满，装填”。

supply [səˈplaɪ] vt. 供应 n. (军队或探险队等的)补给品(supplies)；供应；供应量 1503

词源：late 14c., "to help, support, maintain," also "fill up, make up for," 直接来自拉丁语 supplere "fill up, make full, complete". The meaning "furnish, provide" first recorded 1520s. Related: Supplied; supplying.
助记：sup-(=up from below：从下向上)+ -ply(充满；装填)→从下面补充、填满→供应；补给品

近义词：provide, furnish：供应

搭配：food and petrol supplies：食物和汽油这些补给品

water supply：供水系统

supply the major cities with natural gas：为各主要城市供应天然气

a constant supply of oxygen：持续供氧

in short supply：供应不足

supplier [səˈplaɪə] n. 供应者；供应商 4398
助记：suppli(=supply：供应；补给)+er(名词后缀，表示人)→供应者；供应商

搭配：a leading supplier of computers：一家主要电脑供应商

supplement ['sʌplɪm(ə)nt] vt. 补充 n. 补充；养生片剂；(报纸或杂志的) 增刊 5254
助记：supple(=supply=to fill up：填补)+ -ment(名词后缀)→ 补充；养生片剂；增刊

搭配：supplement their incomes：增加他们的收入

nutritional supplement: 营养补充品

a multiple vitamin and mineral supplement: 一种复合维生素和矿物质养生片剂

a special supplement to a monthly financial magazine: 一份金融月刊的特别增刊

supplementary [,səplɪˈment(ə)rɪ] adj. 补充的；追加的



17005
助记：supplement(补充)+ -ary(形容词后缀)→ 补充的；追加的  

近义词：additional, extra：附加的，额外的  

搭配：take supplementary vitamins：补充维生素  

supplementary information：补充信息





supplemental [,səplɪˈmentəl] adj. 补充的(等于 supplementary) 10545

近义词：supplementary

supplementation [, saplimen'teif[n]] n. 补充；增补；补充营养 18974

complement ['kɒmplɪm(ə)nt] vt. 衬托；补充；使完美 n. 衬托物；补充物 6859
助记：com-(强调前缀)+ -ple-(=to fill：充满)+ -ment(名词后缀)→ 补充填满(之物)→ 衬托；补充；使完美；补充物

搭配：complement the flavor of：凸显...的味道  

the perfect complement to：...的完美衬托  

complement each other：相互取长补短

## 词根 plent-, plen-, plet-=full，表示“大量的；丰盛的”。

plenty ['plenti] quant. 大量的；充裕的 pron. 充裕 adv. 大量；非常 n. 富裕；充裕 2061

词源：13 世纪中期，含义为 "as much as one could desire," 来自古法语 plentee, earlier plentet "abundance, profusion", 来自拉丁语 plenitatem "fullness," from plenus "full, filled, greatly crowded; stout, pregnant; abundant, abounding; complete".
助记：plent-(=full：全体的；全部的)+y→引申词义大量的；充裕；大量；非常

## 词根 pli - 表示“to fill: 装满”。

accomplish [ə'kʌmplɪʃ; ə'kɒm-] vt. 完成 2366

词源：14 世纪后期，“fulfill, perform, carry out an undertaking,” 即含义为“完成，执行，实行某项任务”。来自古法语 acompliss-, acomplir 的现在分词词根，含义为“to fulfill, fill up, complete” (12 世纪现代法语 accomplir). Related: Accomplished; accomplishing.
助记：ac-(=to)+com-(强调前缀)+pli-(=to fill, complete：装满；完成)+-ish (动词后缀)→to fulfill, complete, finish(a task)：完成

近义词：achieve：实现

搭配：mission accomplished：任务完成；大功告成  

accomplish nothing：一事无成

搭配：an accomplished artist/actor/chef：技艺高超的艺术家/演员/厨师  

an elegant and accomplished woman：优雅的才女

accomplishment [ə'kʌmplɪʃm(ə)nt; ə'kɒm-] n. 成就；才艺；完成 4351

搭配：the President's greatest accomplishments：总统最伟大的成就  

a poet of rare accomplishment：出类拔萃的诗人

the accomplishment of our objectives: 实现我们的目标

compliment ['kɒmplɪm(ə)nt] vt. 赞美；称赞；恭维 n. 赞扬；致意；问候 7711

词源：“Act or expression of civility, respect, or regard” (or, as Johnson defines it, "An act, or expression of civility, usually understood to include some hypocrisy, and to mean less than it declares"). By early 19c. the meaning had been extended to "an expression of praise or admiration." 文明，尊敬或尊重的行为或表达(或按照约翰逊的定义，“文明的行为或表达，通常被理解为包含某种虚伪，其含义不如其宣称的那样”)。到 19 世纪初，该含义已扩展为“表达赞美或钦佩”。

搭配：to pay sb. a compliment：对某人表示赞扬  

compliment someone on：称赞某人的...  

My compliments to the chef! 请向厨师代为致意！

辨析：compliment 与 complement

1、compliment 表示赞美、敬佩

She complimented me on my English. 她夸奖我的英语好。

2、complement 表示相辅相成、相配合

The different flavors complement each other perfectly.

不同的味道搭配在一起，可口极了。
