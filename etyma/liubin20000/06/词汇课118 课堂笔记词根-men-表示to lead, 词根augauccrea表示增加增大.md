## 词根-men-表示 to lead, conduct，含义为“引导；行为；举止”。

amenable  $ [əˈmiːnəb(ə)l] $  adj. 顺从的；愿意的；可用某种方式处理的 16327

词源：1590s, "liable to make answer or defense, accountable," 来自盎格鲁-法语 amenable，来自古法语 amener "bring, take, conduct, lead" (to the law), from "to" (see ad-) + mener "to lead", "引导，带领", "tractable=易处理的；易驾驭的"的含义来自1803年, from notion of "disposed to answer or submit to influence", 指“倾向于回答或服从某种影响的”。相关: Amenably.
助记：a-(=to)+men-(=to lead, conduct：引导)+-able(能够...的)→能够被引导做...事情的→顺从的；愿意的；可以用某种方式处理的

搭配：seem amenable to attending a conference：看起来愿意去参加一个会议 very amenable children：很听话的孩子

demeanor [diˈmiːnə] n. 外表；风度；行为；举止 8729
助记：late 15c., demenure, "conduct, management, treatment, behavior toward someone," from obsolete Middle English demean, demeinen "to handle, manage, conduct," later "behave in a certain way, conduct oneself" (early 14c.), from Old French demener (11c.) "to guide, conduct; to live, dwell".
助记：demean(=to conduct：行为；举止)+or(名词后缀)→behave in a certain way, conduct oneself：行为；举止；风度(行为举止给人留下的印象)

搭配：her calm and cheerful demeanor：她那冷静乐观的态度

elegant demeanor：风采

maintain a professional demeanor throughout：始终保持



释义：(N-UNCOUNT)行为；态度 Your demeanor is the way you behave, which gives people an impression of your character and feelings.

词源：“Technique of producing a composite or consecutive whole from fragments of pictures, text, music, etc.,”从图片，文本，音乐等片段中产生合成或连续整体的技术。1929，来自古法语 montage "a mounting," 来自古法语 monter "to go up, mount", 含义为安装，嵌入。Originally a term in cinematography. 原为电影制片术的一个专有名词。

搭配：a photo montage of 一些蒙太奇照片
